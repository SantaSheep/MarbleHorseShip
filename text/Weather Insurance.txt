===Types of Weather Insurance === 
Weather insurance is a type of insurance that provides protection against losses caused by weather-related events. It is designed to protect businesses, farmers, and other individuals from the financial losses that can be caused by extreme weather conditions. Weather insurance can be used to protect against losses due to floods, droughts, hurricanes, and other natural disasters.
