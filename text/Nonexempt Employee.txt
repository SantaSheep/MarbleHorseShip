===Introduction to Nonexempt Employee===

A nonexempt employee is an employee who is not exempt from certain labor laws, such as the Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA). This means that the employee is subject to the minimum wage and overtime requirements of the FLSA. Nonexempt employees are also subject to other labor laws, such as the Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA) and the Occupational Safety and Health Act (OSHA). Nonexempt employees are typically hourly workers, but they can also be salaried employees.
