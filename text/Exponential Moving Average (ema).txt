===Introduction to Exponential Moving Average (EMA) === 
The Exponential Moving Average (EMA) is a technical indicator used in the analysis of financial markets. It is a type of moving average that gives more weight to recent prices in the calculation, making it more sensitive to recent price changes than a simple moving average. The EMA is used to identify trends, spot potential reversals, and provide numerous trading signals.
