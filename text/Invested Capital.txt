===Types of Invested Capital === 
Calculating Invested Capital - Invested capital is calculated by adding up all of the sources of capital that a company has invested in its operations. This includes debt, equity, and other investments. The total amount of invested capital is then divided by the total number of shares outstanding to calculate the invested capital per share.

===Benefits of Invested Capital === 
Conclusion - Invested capital is an important component of a company's financial health and can provide it with the funds it needs to grow and generate returns on its investments. However, it is important to understand the risks associated with invested capital and to diversify sources of capital to reduce risk.
