===Introduction to GMROI===
[[Gross Margin|Gross Margin]] [[Return On Investment|Return on Investment]] (GMROI) is a financial metric used to measure the profitability of a company’s investments. GMROI is a useful tool for investors and business owners to assess the performance of their investments and to make decisions about future investments.

GMROI is defined as the ratio of gross margin to total investment. It is calculated by dividing the gross margin by the total investment. The total investment is the sum of all investments made by the company, including capital investments, operating expenses, and other investments.
