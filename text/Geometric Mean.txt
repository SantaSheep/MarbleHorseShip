===Introduction to Geometric Mean === 
Geometric mean is a type of average used to measure the central tendency of a set of numbers. It is calculated by taking the product of all the numbers in the set and then taking the nth root of the product, where n is the number of numbers in the set. Geometric mean is often used to measure the rate of return of an investment over a period of time.
