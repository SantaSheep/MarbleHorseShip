===Introduction to Corteva===

Corteva is a global agricultural company that provides products and services to farmers and other agricultural stakeholders. The company was formed in 2019 through the merger of DowDuPont’s Agriculture Division and Corteva Agriscience. Corteva is headquartered in Wilmington, Delaware and has operations in more than 140 countries. The company’s mission is to “help farmers around the world grow more, healthier and more sustainable food, feed, fiber and fuel.”
