===Introduction to Logistics===

Logistics is the process of planning, organizing, and managing the efficient flow of goods, services, and information from the point of origin to the point of consumption. Logistics is a critical component of any business, as it is responsible for the efficient and cost-effective movement of goods and services from one point to another. Logistics is a complex process that involves the coordination of multiple activities, including transportation, warehousing, [[Inventory Management|inventory management]], and [[Customer Service|customer service]]. Logistics is a key factor in the success of any business, as it can have a significant impact on the cost, quality, and speed of delivery of goods and services.
