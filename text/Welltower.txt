===Introduction to Welltower===

Welltower Inc. is a [[Real Estate|real estate]] investment trust (REIT) that specializes in healthcare-related properties. Founded in 1970, the company is headquartered in Toledo, Ohio and is one of the largest REITs in the United States. Welltower owns and operates a portfolio of over 1,400 properties across the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom. The company’s portfolio includes senior housing, post-acute care, and outpatient medical properties. Welltower is publicly traded on the New York Stock Exchange under the ticker symbol “HCN”.
