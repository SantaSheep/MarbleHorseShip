===Introduction to General Motors===

General Motors (GM) is an American [[Multinational Corporation|multinational corporation]] that designs, manufactures, markets, and distributes vehicles and vehicle parts, and sells financial services. GM is the world’s largest automaker by vehicle [[Unit Sales|unit sales]], and is the second-largest automaker in the world by revenue. GM is headquartered in Detroit, Michigan, and employs over 180,000 people in its global operations. GM is a publicly traded company, and its shares are listed on the New York Stock Exchange.
===History of General Motors===

GM was founded in 1908 by William  Durant, who had previously founded the Chevrolet Motor Company. GM was initially formed as a [[Holding Company|holding company]] for Durant’s various automotive interests, and quickly grew to become one of the largest automakers in the world. GM has since gone through several major reorganizations, including a bankruptcy in 2009. In 2010, GM emerged from bankruptcy and began to focus on its core automotive business.
