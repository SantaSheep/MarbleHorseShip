===Introduction to Walgreens Boots Alliance===

Walgreens Boots Alliance (WBA) is a global pharmacy-led health and wellbeing enterprise. It is the largest retail pharmacy, health and daily living destination in the United States. WBA operates more than 18,500 stores in 11 countries, including the United States, the United Kingdom, Ireland, Mexico, Switzerland, Norway, and Thailand. The company is headquartered in Deerfield, Illinois. WBA is a publicly traded company listed on the NASDAQ stock exchange.
===History of Walgreens Boots Alliance===

WBA was formed in 2014 through the merger of Walgreens and Alliance Boots. Walgreens was founded in 1901 by Charles R. Walgreen, Sr. in Chicago, Illinois. The company grew to become the largest drugstore chain in the United States. Alliance Boots was founded in 2005 through the merger of Alliance UniChem and Boots Group. Alliance UniChem was founded in 1977 and Boots Group was founded in 1849. The merger of Walgreens and Alliance Boots created a global pharmacy-led health and wellbeing enterprise.
===Business Model of Walgreens Boots Alliance===

WBA operates three business segments: Retail Pharmacy USA, Retail Pharmacy International, and Pharmaceutical Wholesale. The Retail Pharmacy USA segment operates retail drugstores in the United States. The Retail Pharmacy International segment operates retail drugstores in the United Kingdom, Ireland, Mexico, Switzerland, Norway, and Thailand. The Pharmaceutical Wholesale segment distributes pharmaceuticals and other health and beauty products to pharmacies, doctors, health centers, and hospitals.
