===Inc.===

F5,  is a global leader in application delivery networking technology. Founded in 1996, the company is headquartered in Seattle, Washington and has offices in more than 50 countries around the world. F5 provides a wide range of products and services that enable organizations to securely deliver applications, data, and services to users across the globe. F5’s solutions are used by some of the world’s largest companies, including Microsoft, Amazon, and Google.

History of F5, 

F5 was founded in 1996 by CEO John McAdam and CTO Jeff Hussey. The company was initially focused on providing web-based application delivery solutions. Over the years, F5 has grown to become a leader in the application delivery networking space. The company has acquired several companies, including NGINX, Shape Security, and A10 Networks, to expand its product portfolio and customer base. F5 has also invested heavily in research and development, resulting in the development of innovative products and services.
