===Types of Exchange Ratios === 
Exchange ratio is a financial metric used to compare the relative value of two companies. It is calculated by dividing the market capitalization of one company by the market capitalization of the other. Exchange ratios are used to measure the relative size of two companies and to compare their financial performance.
