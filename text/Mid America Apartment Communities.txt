===Introduction to Mid America Apartment Communities===
Mid America Apartment Communities (MAA) is a [[Real Estate|real estate]] investment trust (REIT) that owns and operates multifamily apartment communities in the United States. The company was founded in 1994 and is headquartered in Memphis, Tennessee. MAA owns and operates over 100,000 apartment units in 17 states and is one of the largest publicly traded REITs in the United States. MAA focuses on providing quality housing to its tenants and is committed to providing a safe and comfortable living environment.
===History of Mid America Apartment Communities===
MAA was founded in 1994 by Chairman and CEO, Eric Bolton. The company was initially focused on acquiring and managing apartment communities in the Southeast United States. Over the years, MAA has expanded its portfolio to include properties in 17 states across the United States. MAA has also expanded its services to include property management, development, and construction services.
