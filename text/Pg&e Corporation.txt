===Introduction to PG&E Corporation===

PG&E Corporation is a publicly traded energy [[Holding Company|holding company]] headquartered in San Francisco, California. It is the [[Parent Company|parent company]] of Pacific Gas and Electric Company, a regulated utility that provides natural gas and electric service to approximately 16 million people in Northern and Central California. PG&E Corporation is listed on the New York Stock Exchange under the ticker symbol PCG. The company is a major player in the energy industry, with a [[Market Capitalization|market capitalization]] of over $30 billion.
===History of PG&E Corporation===

PG&E Corporation was founded in 1905 as the Pacific Gas and Electric Company. The company was created to provide electricity and natural gas to the growing population of California. Over the years, the company has grown to become one of the largest energy companies in the United States. In 1997, the company was reorganized as a holding company and renamed PG&E Corporation.
