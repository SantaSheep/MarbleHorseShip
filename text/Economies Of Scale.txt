===Types of Economies of Scale === 
Benefits of Economies of Scale: The main benefit of economies of scale is that it allows businesses to produce more goods at a lower cost. This can lead to increased profits, as well as increased market share and competitive advantage. Additionally, economies of scale can lead to increased efficiency, as businesses can produce more goods with fewer resources.
