===Introduction to Boston Properties===

Boston Properties is a [[Real Estate|real estate]] investment trust (REIT) that owns, develops, and manages office, residential, and retail properties in the United States. The company was founded in 1970 and is headquartered in Boston, Massachusetts. Boston Properties is one of the largest publicly traded REITs in the United States and is listed on the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE) under the ticker symbol BXP.
===History of Boston Properties===

Boston Properties was founded in 1970 by Mortimer B. Zuckerman and Edward H. Linde. The company was initially focused on the development of office properties in the Boston area. Over the years, the company has expanded its portfolio to include properties in other major cities in the United States, such as New York City, Washington , San Francisco, and Los Angeles.
