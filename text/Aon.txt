===Introduction to Aon===
Aon is a leading global professional services firm providing a broad range of risk, retirement and health solutions. Aon’s approximately 50,000 colleagues in 120 countries empower results for clients by using proprietary data and analytics to deliver insights that reduce volatility and improve performance. Aon was founded in 1919 and is headquartered in London, England.
===History of Aon===
Aon was founded in 1919 by Arthur E. Anderson and Cornelius Vander Starr. Anderson and Starr had previously worked together at the insurance brokerage firm of Starr & Company. Anderson and Starr saw an opportunity to create a new type of insurance brokerage firm that could provide a more comprehensive range of services to its clients. In 1922, Anderson and Starr established the first international office in Shanghai, China. Since then, Aon has grown to become one of the largest and most successful professional services firms in the world.

Aon’s [[Financial Performance|financial performance]] has been strong over the past several years. In 2019, Aon reported total revenue of $13.2 billion, an increase of 8.3% from the prior year. Aon also provides a range of technology-enabled services, such as data and analytics, to help its clients manage their risk and optimize their performance.
