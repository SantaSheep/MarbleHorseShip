===Introduction to Accounts Payable Turnover Ratio === 
Accounts payable turnover ratio is a financial metric used to measure the efficiency of a company’s accounts payable process. It is calculated by dividing the total amount of accounts payable by the average amount of accounts payable outstanding during a given period. This ratio is used to assess the liquidity of a company’s accounts payable and to determine how quickly a company is paying its suppliers.
