===Introduction to Texas Instruments===

Texas Instruments (TI) is a global semiconductor design and manufacturing company based in Dallas, Texas. Founded in 1930, TI is one of the world’s largest semiconductor companies and is a leader in the development of analog and embedded processing technologies. TI’s products are used in a wide range of applications, including automotive, industrial, medical, and consumer electronics. ===History of Texas Instruments===

In the 1960s, TI developed the first integrated circuit and the first single-chip microprocessor. In the 1970s, TI developed the first digital signal processor and the first single-chip calculator.
