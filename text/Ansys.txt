===Introduction to Ansys===
Ansys is a global leader in engineering simulation software. Founded in 1970, Ansys has grown to become a leading provider of engineering simulation solutions for a wide range of industries, including automotive, aerospace, defense, electronics, energy, and healthcare. Ansys’ products and services are used by engineers and scientists to design, analyze, and optimize products and processes. Ansys’ solutions are used to simulate the behavior of complex systems, such as aircraft, automobiles, and medical devices, and to optimize their performance. Ansys’ products and services are used by customers in more than 40 countries around the world.
===History of Ansys===
Ansys was founded in 1970 by John Swanson, a professor of electrical engineering at Carnegie Mellon University. Swanson developed the first version of Ansys’ flagship product, Ansys Mechanical, in the early 1970s. Since then, Ansys has grown to become a leading provider of engineering simulation solutions. Ansys has acquired several companies over the years, including Fluent Inc. in 2006, CFX in 2008, and SpaceClaim in 2013. Ansys has also developed several new products, such as Ansys Discovery, Ansys Fluent, and Ansys HFSS.
===Products and Services Offered by Ansys===
Ansys offers a wide range of products and services for engineering simulation. Ansys’ flagship product, Ansys Mechanical, is a general-purpose finite element analysis (FEA) software package used to simulate the behavior of complex systems. Ansys also offers several other products, such as Ansys Discovery, Ansys Fluent, and Ansys HFSS. Ansys also offers a range of services, such as consulting, training, and support.
