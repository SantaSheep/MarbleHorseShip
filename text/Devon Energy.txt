===Introduction to Devon Energy===

Devon Energy is an American energy company based in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma. It is one of the largest independent oil and natural gas exploration and production companies in the United States. Devon Energy is engaged in the exploration, development, production, and marketing of oil, natural gas, and natural gas liquids. The company operates in the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom.
===History of Devon Energy===

Devon Energy was founded in 1971 by John Nichols and Larry Nichols. The company was initially focused on oil and gas exploration and production in the United States. In the 1980s, Devon Energy began to expand its operations into Canada and the United Kingdom. In the 1990s, the company began to focus on natural gas production and exploration. In 2000, Devon Energy acquired Mitchell Energy & Development Corporation, which was a major player in the natural gas industry.
