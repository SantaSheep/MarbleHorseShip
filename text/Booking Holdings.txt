===Introduction to Booking Holdings===
Booking Holdings is a global leader in online travel and related services. It is the world’s largest online travel company, with a portfolio of brands that includes Booking.com, Priceline.com, Agoda.com, Kayak.com, Rentalcars.com, and OpenTable. The company is headquartered in Norwalk, Connecticut, and has offices in more than 200 countries and territories. Booking Holdings is a publicly traded company listed on the NASDAQ Global Select Market under the ticker symbol BKNG.
===History of Booking Holdings===
Booking Holdings was founded in 1997 as The Priceline Group. The company was initially focused on providing online travel services, such as airline tickets, hotel reservations, and car rentals. In 2005, the company acquired Booking.com, a European-based online travel agency, and rebranded itself as The Priceline Group. In 2013, the company changed its name to Booking Holdings to better reflect its global presence and portfolio of brands.
===Business Model of Booking Holdings===
Booking Holdings’ business model is based on providing customers with access to a wide range of travel services, including flights, hotels, car rentals, and vacation packages. The company’s core services are provided through its portfolio of brands, which include Booking.com, Priceline.com, Agoda.com, Kayak.com, Rentalcars.com, and OpenTable. The company also offers a variety of other services, such as travel insurance, airport transfers, and activities.
