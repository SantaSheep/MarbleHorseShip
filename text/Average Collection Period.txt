===Introduction to Average Collection Period === 
The average collection period is a financial metric used to measure the average amount of time it takes a company to collect payments from its customers. It is an important indicator of a company’s liquidity and cash flow, and can be used to assess the effectiveness of a company’s credit and collection policies.
