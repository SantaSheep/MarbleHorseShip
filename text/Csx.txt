===Introduction to CSX===
CSX Corporation, commonly known as CSX, is an American publicly traded railroad and transportation company headquartered in Jacksonville, Florida. CSX is one of the largest railroads in the United States, operating over 21,000 miles of track in 23 states, the District of Columbia, and the Canadian provinces of Ontario and Quebec. CSX is a major transporter of coal, automotive, and industrial products, and also provides intermodal and rail-to-truck transloading services.

CSX was founded in 1978 as a result of the merger of Chessie System and Seaboard Coast Line Industries. The merger was approved by the Interstat[[E Commerce|e Commerce]] Commission in 1980, and the new company was named CSX Corporation.
