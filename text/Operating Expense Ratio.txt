===Introduction to Operating Expense Ratio === 
Operating expense ratio (OER) is a financial metric used to measure the efficiency of a company’s operations. It is calculated by dividing the total operating expenses of a company by its total revenue. OER is a useful tool for investors and analysts to assess the financial health of a company and compare it to its peers.
