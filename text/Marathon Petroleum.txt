===Introduction to Marathon Petroleum===

Marathon Petroleum Corporation is an American petroleum refining, marketing, and transportation company headquartered in Findlay, Ohio. It is the largest petroleum refiner in the United States and the fourth largest in the world. Marathon Petroleum is a Fortune 500 company and is a subsidiary of [[Marathon Oil|Marathon Oil]] Corporation. Marathon Petroleum is a major player in the oil and gas industry, with operations in the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom.
===History of Marathon Petroleum===

Marathon Petroleum was founded in 1887 as the Ohio Oil Company. The company was initially focused on the production and sale of petroleum products in the Midwest. In 1930, the company changed its name to Marathon Oil Company and began to expand its operations to other parts of the United States. In 1962, Marathon Oil Company merged with Ashland Oil and Refining Company to form Marathon Ashland Petroleum LLC. In 2005, Marathon Ashland Petroleum LLC was renamed Marathon Petroleum Corporation.
