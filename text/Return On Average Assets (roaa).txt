===Introduction to Return on Average Assets (ROAA) === 
Return on Average Assets (ROAA) is a financial ratio that measures the profitability of a company relative to its total assets. It is calculated by dividing the net income of a company by its average total assets. ROAA is a useful metric for investors and analysts to assess the overall performance of a company and its ability to generate profits from its assets.
