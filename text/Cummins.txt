===Introduction to Cummins===

Cummins Inc. is an American Fortune 500 corporation that designs, manufactures, and distributes engines, filtration, and power generation products. Founded in 1919, Cummins is one of the world’s largest producers of diesel engines and related components. The company has operations in more than 190 countries and territories and employs over 60,000 people worldwide. Cummins’ products are used in a variety of applications, including on-highway, off-highway, construction, mining, marine, power generation, and industrial.
