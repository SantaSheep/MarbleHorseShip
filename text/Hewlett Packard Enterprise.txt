===Introduction to Hewlett Packard Enterprise===

Hewlett Packard Enterprise (HPE) is a global technology company that provides a wide range of products and services to its customers. HPE is a leader in the enterprise technology space, offering solutions for data centers, [[Cloud Computing|cloud computing]], software, storage, and networking. HPE is headquartered in Palo Alto, California and has operations in over 170 countries. HPE is a publicly traded company on the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE) and is a component of the S&P 500 index.
===History of Hewlett Packard Enterprise===

HPE was founded in 1939 by William Hewlett and David Packard. The two engineers met while studying at Stanford University and decided to start a business together. They initially started out in a garage in Palo Alto, California and developed a number of products, including the first audio oscillator. HPE went public in 1957 and has since grown to become one of the largest technology companies in the world.
===Business Model of Hewlett Packard Enterprise===

HPE’s business model is focused on providing enterprise technology solutions to its customers. HPE offers a wide range of products and services, including servers, storage, networking, software, and cloud computing. HPE also provides consulting and support services to its customers. HPE’s customers include large enterprises, small and medium businesses, and government agencies.
