===Introduction to Growth Rates === 
Growth rates are a measure of the rate of change in a given variable over a period of time. They are used to measure the performance of a company, industry, or economy. Growth rates can be used to compare the performance of different companies or industries, or to measure the performance of a single company or industry over time. Growth rates can also be used to forecast future performance.
