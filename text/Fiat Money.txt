===Introduction to Fiat Money === 
Fiat money is a type of currency that is issued by a government and declared to be legal tender. It is not backed by a physical commodity such as gold or silver, but rather by the government that issued it. Fiat money is also known as fiat currency or paper money. It is the most common form of currency in circulation today.
