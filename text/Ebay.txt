===Introduction to Ebay===

Ebay is an American multinational e-commerce corporation based in San Jose, California. Founded in 1995, Ebay is one of the world’s largest online marketplaces, allowing buyers and sellers to connect and transact with each other. Ebay’s platform enables users to buy and sell a wide variety of goods and services, including electronics, apparel, collectibles, home goods, and more. Ebay also offers a variety of services, such as payment processing, shipping, and [[Customer Service|customer service]]. Ebay is a publicly traded company, listed on the Nasdaq stock exchange.
===History of Ebay===

Ebay was founded in 1995 by Pierre Omidyar, an Iranian-American computer programmer. Omidyar had the idea to create an online marketplace where people could buy and sell items. He launched the website in September 1995, and it quickly gained traction. By 1997, Ebay had become one of the most popular online marketplaces, and it went public in 1998.
===Ebay’s Business Model===

Ebay’s business model is based on a commission-based system. Ebay charges a fee for each transaction that takes place on its platform. This fee is typically a percentage of the total sale price. Ebay also charges fees for listing items, as well as for additional services such as payment processing and shipping. Ebay also offers advertising services, which generate additional revenue.
===Ebay’s Financial Performance===

Ebay’s [[Financial Performance|financial performance]] has been strong in recent years. In 2020, Ebay reported total revenue of $10.7 billion, up from $10.3 billion in 2019. Ebay’s [[Net Income|net income]] for 2020 was $2.2 billion, up from $1.9 billion in 2019. Ebay’s stock price has also been steadily increasing, reaching an all-time high of $64.99 in February 2021.
===Ebay’s Impact on the Economy===

Ebay has had a significant impact on the global economy. Ebay’s platform has enabled millions of people to buy and sell goods and services, creating economic opportunities for both buyers and sellers. Ebay has also created jobs, both directly and indirectly, and has helped to stimulate [[Economic Growth|economic growth]] in many countries.
