===Introduction to Amgen===
Amgen is a biotechnology company that is headquartered in Thousand Oaks, California. Founded in 1980, Amgen is one of the world’s largest independent biotechnology companies. ===History of Amgen===
Amgen was founded in 1980 by [[Venture Capital|venture capital]]ists William K. Bowes Jr. and George B. Rathmann. The company was initially focused on developing recombinant DNA technology to produce human proteins in bacteria. In 1983, Amgen became the first company to successfully produce a recombinant human protein in bacteria. This breakthrough enabled the company to develop a range of therapeutic proteins, including erythropoietin, which is used to treat anemia.
