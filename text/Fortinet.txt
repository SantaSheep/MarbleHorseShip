===Introduction to Fortinet===
Fortinet is a global leader in network security, providing comprehensive protection for organizations of all sizes. Founded in 2000, Fortinet has grown to become one of the world’s largest providers of network security solutions. Fortinet’s products and services are designed to protect organizations from the ever-evolving threats posed by cyber criminals. Fortinet’s solutions are used by organizations in a variety of industries, including government, healthcare, finance, and education.
===History of Fortinet===
Fortinet was founded in 2000 by Ken Xie, a computer scientist and entrepreneur. Xie had previously founded NetScreen Technologies, a network security company that was acquired by [[Juniper Networks|Juniper Networks]] in 2004. After the acquisition, Xie founded Fortinet with the goal of providing comprehensive network security solutions. Since its founding, Fortinet has grown to become one of the world’s largest providers of network security solutions.
===Products and Services Offered by Fortinet===
Fortinet offers a wide range of products and services designed to protect organizations from cyber threats. These products and services include firewalls, unified threat management (UTM) solutions, secure web gateways, secure email gateways, and secure access solutions. Fortinet also offers a range of managed security services, including managed firewall, managed UTM, and managed secure web gateway services.
