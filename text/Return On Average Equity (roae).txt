===Introduction to Return on Average Equity (ROAE) === 
Return on Average Equity (ROAE) is a financial ratio that measures the profitability of a company relative to its shareholders’ equity. It is calculated by dividing the company’s net income by its average shareholders’ equity over a certain period of time. ROAE is a useful tool for investors to assess the performance of a company and compare it to other companies in the same industry.
