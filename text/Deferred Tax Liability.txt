===Advantages and Disadvantages of Deferred Tax Liability === 
Advantages and Disadvantages of Deferred Tax Liability - The main advantage of deferred tax liabilities is that they can help a company to reduce its current tax liability. This can be beneficial in the short term, as it can help to reduce the amount of taxes that the company has to pay in the current period. However, there are also some disadvantages to deferred tax liabilities. For example, they can create a large liability that must be paid in the future, which can be difficult to manage.
