===Introduction to Public Service Enterprise Group===

Public Service Enterprise Group (PSEG) is a publicly traded energy company headquartered in Newark, New Jersey. It is the [[Parent Company|parent company]] of Public Service Electric and Gas Company (PSE&G), a regulated electric and gas utility in New Jersey. PSEG also owns and operates power plants, provides energy services, and engages in energy-related businesses. The company is one of the largest energy companies in the United States and is listed on the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE) under the ticker symbol PEG.
===History of Public Service Enterprise Group===

Public Service Enterprise Group was founded in 1903 as the Public Service Corporation of New Jersey. The company was formed to provide electric and gas services to customers in New Jersey. In 1981, the company changed its name to Public Service Electric and Gas Company (PSE&G). In 1985, the company was reorganized as a [[Holding Company|holding company]] and changed its name to Public Service Enterprise Group.
===Business Model of Public Service Enterprise Group===

Public Service Enterprise Group operates in three main business segments: regulated electric and gas utility, power generation, and energy services. The regulated electric and gas utility segment provides electric and gas services to customers in New Jersey. The power generation segment owns and operates power plants in the United States and Canada. The energy services segment provides energy-related services such as energy efficiency, demand response, and renewable energy.
