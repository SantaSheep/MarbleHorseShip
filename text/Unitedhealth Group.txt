===Introduction to Unitedhealth Group===
UnitedHealth Group is a diversified health and well-being company that serves more than 115 million customers worldwide. It is one of the largest health care companies in the United States and is a Fortune 500 company. UnitedHealth Group provides a wide range of services, including health care coverage, health care management, and health care technology. The company operates through four main business segments: UnitedHealthcare, Optum, UnitedHealth Group Ventures, and UnitedHealth Group International.
===History of Unitedhealth Group===
UnitedHealth Group was founded in 1977 as United HealthCare Corporation. The company was initially focused on providing health care coverage to individuals and families. In the 1980s, the company expanded its services to include health care management and technology. In the 1990s, the company began to expand its operations internationally and acquired several companies, including Oxford Health Plans and MetraHealth. In the 2000s, the company continued to expand its operations and acquired several more companies, including AmeriChoice and Ingenix.
