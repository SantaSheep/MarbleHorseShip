===Introduction to Operating Activities === 
Operating activities are the activities that are related to the day-to-day operations of a business. These activities include the production of goods and services, the sale of goods and services, and the collection of payments from customers. Operating activities are the primary source of revenue for a business and are essential for its success.
===Cash Flow from Operating Activities === 
Cash flow from operating activities is the amount of cash generated from the day-to-day operations of a business. This includes cash received from customers for goods and services, cash paid to suppliers for goods and services, and cash paid to employees for wages and salaries.
