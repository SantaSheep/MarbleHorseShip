===Introduction to Ex-Post Risk === 

===Types of Ex-Post Risk === 
Ex-post risk is a type of financial risk that is associated with the performance of investments after they have been made. It is the risk that the return on an investment will be lower than expected or that the investment will not perform as expected. Ex-post risk is also known as post-investment risk or post-acquisition risk. It is important for investors to understand ex-post risk and how to manage it in order to maximize their returns.
