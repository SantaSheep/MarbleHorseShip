===Introduction to ConocoPhillips===

ConocoPhillips is an American multinational energy corporation headquartered in Houston, Texas. It is the world’s largest independent exploration and production company and the third-largest integrated energy company in the United States. The company operates in more than 30 countries and has a presence in all of the world’s major oil and gas regions. ConocoPhillips is engaged in the exploration, production, transportation, and marketing of oil and natural gas. It also produces and markets petrochemicals, lubricants, and other specialty products.

ConocoPhillips was formed in 2002 through the merger of Conoco Inc. and Phillips Petroleum Company. Conoco Inc. was founded in 1875 as the Continental Oil and Transportation Company.
