===Definition of Hurdle Rate === 
Factors that Influence Hurdle Rate - The hurdle rate is influenced by a variety of factors, including the investor's risk tolerance, the expected return of the project, the cost of capital, the expected inflation rate, and the expected rate of return of alternative investments.

===Calculating Hurdle Rate === 
Conclusion - Hurdle rate is a financial concept used to determine the minimum rate of return a project must generate in order for it to be considered a viable investment. It is used to compare the expected return of a project to the risk associated with it, and helps investors make informed decisions about their investments.
