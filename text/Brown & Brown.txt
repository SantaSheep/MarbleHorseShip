===Introduction to Brown & Brown===
Brown & Brown is a publicly traded insurance brokerage and consulting firm based in Daytona Beach, Florida. Founded in 1939, the company has grown to become one of the largest insurance intermediaries in the United States. Brown & Brown provides a wide range of insurance and [[Risk Management|risk management]] services to businesses, government entities, and individuals. The company operates through a network of more than 200 offices located in 39 states and the District of Columbia.
===History of Brown & Brown===
Brown & Brown was founded in 1939 by J. Adrian Brown and his brother, Hyman Brown. The brothers began their business by selling [[Life Insurance|life insurance]] policies door-to-door in Daytona Beach, Florida. In the 1950s, the company began to expand its services to include property and casualty insurance. In the 1960s, the company began to offer employee benefits and risk management services. In the 1970s, the company began to expand its geographic reach by opening offices in other states. In the 1980s, the company began to acquire other insurance agencies and expand its services to include surety bonds, financial services, and reinsurance.
