===Introduction to NRG Energy===

NRG Energy is an American energy company that is headquartered in Princeton, New Jersey. It is one of the largest independent power producers in the United States, with a portfolio of over 50,000 megawatts of generation capacity. NRG Energy is a publicly traded company on the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE: NRG). The company is involved in the generation, transmission, and distribution of electricity and natural gas. It also provides energy services to residential, commercial, and industrial customers.
===History of NRG Energy===

NRG Energy was founded in 1989 as a spin-off of the [[Public Service Enterprise Group|Public Service Enterprise Group]] (PSEG). The company was initially focused on the generation of electricity from natural gas and coal-fired power plants. In the early 2000s, NRG Energy began to expand its operations into the retail electricity market. In 2006, the company acquired Reliant Energy, which was one of the largest retail electricity providers in the United States. In 2009, NRG Energy acquired the retail electricity business of [[Xcel Energy|Xcel Energy]], which further expanded its retail electricity operations.
