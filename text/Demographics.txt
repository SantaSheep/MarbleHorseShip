===Introduction to Demographics === 
Demographics are the characteristics of a population that are used to describe and analyze a population. Demographics are used to measure the size, composition, and distribution of a population. Demographics are used to understand the population’s needs and to plan for the future. Demographics are used to understand the population’s characteristics, such as age, gender, race, ethnicity, education, and income.
===Population Growth === 
Population growth is the change in the size of a population over time. Population growth can be measured by the number of people added to or subtracted from a population. Population growth can be caused by natural factors, such as birth and death rates, or by human factors, such as immigration and emigration.
