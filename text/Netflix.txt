===Introduction to Netflix===
Netflix is an American media-services provider and production company founded in 1997 by Reed Hastings and Marc Randolph. It is one of the world’s leading streaming entertainment services, with over 167 million paid subscribers in over 190 countries. Netflix offers a wide variety of content, including original series, films, documentaries, and stand-up comedy specials. Netflix is headquartered in Los Gatos, California, and is available on a variety of devices, including smart TVs, game consoles, streaming media players, and mobile devices.
===History of Netflix===
Netflix was founded in 1997 by Reed Hastings and Marc Randolph. The two had previously founded Pure Software, a software development company, and wanted to create a new business model for the distribution of movies and television shows. Netflix began as a DVD-by-mail rental service, allowing customers to rent DVDs and have them delivered to their homes. In 1999, Netflix launched its streaming service, allowing customers to watch movies and television shows online. In 2007, Netflix began offering streaming content to customers in the United States. Since then, Netflix has expanded its streaming service to over 190 countries and has become one of the world’s leading streaming entertainment services.
===Netflix Business Model===
Netflix’s business model is based on subscription-based streaming. Customers pay a monthly fee to access Netflix’s library of content, which includes original series, films, documentaries, and stand-up comedy specials. Netflix also offers a variety of other services, such as DVD rentals, Blu-ray rentals, and video game rentals. Netflix also offers a variety of add-on services, such as the ability to download content for offline viewing and the ability to watch content in 4K Ultra HD.
===Netflix Financial Performance===
Netflix has seen tremendous growth in recent years. In 2020, Netflix reported revenue of $25.9 billion, an increase of 24% from the previous year. Netflix’s subscriber base has also grown significantly, with 167 million paid subscribers in over 190 countries.

Netflix has had a significant impact on the entertainment industry. Netflix has also become a major player in the production of original content, producing a variety of critically acclaimed series, films, and documentaries.
