===History of Keycorp===
Keycorp was founded in 1825 as the Commercial Bank of Cleveland. In 1848, the bank changed its name to the National City Bank of Cleveland. In 1968, the bank merged with the Society for Savings and changed its name to KeyCorp. In 1994, KeyCorp acquired the First Security Corporation, a Utah-based bank, and changed its name to KeyBank. In 1998, KeyCorp acquired the McDonald & Company Investments, Inc., a Cleveland-based investment banking firm.
