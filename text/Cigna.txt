===Introduction to Cigna===

Cigna is a global health service company that provides health, dental, vision, and [[Life Insurance|life insurance]] products and services to individuals, families, and businesses. Founded in 1982, Cigna is headquartered in Bloomfield, Connecticut and operates in over 30 countries. Cigna is one of the largest health insurance companies in the United States, with over 15 million customers. Cigna’s mission is to improve the health, well-being, and peace of mind of those it serves.
===History of Cigna===

Cigna was founded in 1982 as a merger between Connecticut General Corporation and INA Corporation. The company was initially focused on providing health insurance to individuals and businesses. In the 1990s, Cigna expanded its business to include life insurance, dental insurance, and vision insurance. In the 2000s, Cigna began to expand its international presence, entering markets in Europe, Asia, and Latin America. In 2018, Cigna acquired Express Scripts, a pharmacy benefit manager, for $67 billion.
===Cigna’s Business Model===

Cigna’s business model is based on providing health, dental, vision, and life insurance products and services to individuals, families, and businesses. Cigna also provides pharmacy benefit management services through its Express Scripts subsidiary. Cigna’s products and services are sold through a network of independent agents, brokers, and employers.
===Cigna’s Financial Performance===

Cigna’s [[Financial Performance|financial performance]] has been strong in recent years. In 2019, Cigna reported revenue of $154.2 billion, up from $146.2 billion in 2018. [[Net Income|Net income]] for 2019 was $3.9 billion, up from $3.3 billion in 2018. Cigna’s stock price has also been strong, rising from $150 per share in January 2019 to $213 per share in January 2020.
===Cigna’s Impact on the Healthcare Industry===

Cigna has had a significant impact on the healthcare industry. Cigna’s acquisition of Express Scripts in 2018 has allowed the company to expand its pharmacy benefit management services and provide more comprehensive healthcare services to its customers. Cigna has also been a leader in the development of innovative healthcare products and services, such as its telemedicine services and its mobile app.
