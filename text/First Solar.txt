===Introduction to First Solar===

First Solar is a leading global provider of comprehensive photovoltaic (PV) solar energy solutions. Founded in 1999, the company designs, manufactures, and sells PV solar modules, which convert sunlight into electricity. First Solar is headquartered in Tempe, Arizona, and has operations in the United States, Europe, India, and China. The company is publicly traded on the NASDAQ stock exchange under the ticker symbol FSLR.
===History of First Solar===

First Solar was founded in 1999 by Michael Ahearn and Harold McMaster. Ahearn and McMaster had previously founded a company called Solar Cells, Inc., which was the first company to produce thin-film solar cells. In 2000, First Solar acquired Solar Cells, Inc. and began to focus on the development and production of thin-film solar modules.

In 2006, First Solar became the first solar company to be listed on the NASDAQ stock exchange. Since then, the company has grown rapidly, expanding its operations to include manufacturing facilities in the United States, Europe, India, and China. In 2011, First Solar acquired the solar energy company OptiSolar, which further expanded the company’s reach in the solar energy industry.
