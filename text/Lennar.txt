===Introduction to Lennar===

Lennar Corporation is a leading homebuilder in the United States. Founded in 1954, the company has grown to become one of the largest homebuilders in the country, with operations in over 50 markets across the United States. Lennar is a publicly traded company on the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE: LEN) and is a component of the S&P 500 index. The company is headquartered in Miami, Florida and is led by Chairman and CEO Stuart Miller.
===History of Lennar===

Lennar was founded in 1954 by Leonard Miller and Arnold Rosen in Miami, Florida. The company began as a small homebuilding business and quickly grew to become one of the largest homebuilders in the United States. In the early years, Lennar focused on building single-family homes in the Miami area. Over the years, the company expanded its operations to include multi-family homes, condominiums, and townhomes. In the late 1990s, Lennar began to expand its operations to other parts of the country, including California, Arizona, and Texas.
===Financial Performance of Lennar===

Lennar has had a strong [[Financial Performance|financial performance]] over the years. In 2019, the company reported total revenues of $17.3 billion, up from $15.7 billion in 2018. [[Net Income|Net income]] for the year was $1.2 billion, up from $1.1 billion in 2018. The company’s total assets at the end of 2019 were $20.3 billion, up from $18.7 billion in 2018. Lennar’s stock price has also been strong, with the stock price increasing from $45.50 in January 2019 to $68.50 in December 2019.
===Corporate Structure of Lennar===

Lennar is organized into four main divisions: Homebuilding, Financial Services, Rialto, and Lennar Multifamily. The Homebuilding division is responsible for the construction and sale of single-family homes, townhomes, and condominiums. The Financial Services division provides mortgage financing, title insurance, and other related services. The Rialto division is focused on [[Real Estate|real estate]] investments and [[Asset Management|asset management]]. The Lennar Multifamily division is focused on the development and management of multifamily rental properties.
