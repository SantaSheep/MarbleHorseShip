===What is a Hammer Candlestick? === 
A Hammer candlestick is a single candlestick pattern that is used to identify potential reversals in the market. It is characterized by a small real body, a long lower shadow, and a short or non-existent upper shadow. The long lower shadow indicates that the price has been pushed down during the period, but then buyers stepped in and pushed the price back up. This indicates that the buyers are in control and that the trend may be reversing.
