===Introduction to Standard Deviation === 
Standard deviation is a measure of the spread of a set of data from its mean. It is a measure of how much variation or dispersion exists from the average or mean value. It is used to measure the volatility of a stock, the risk of a portfolio, or the variability of a population. Standard deviation is a key concept in finance and is used to measure the risk of an investment.
