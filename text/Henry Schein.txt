===Introduction to Henry Schein===

Henry Schein, Inc. is a Fortune 500 company and the world’s largest provider of health care products and services to office-based dental, animal health, and medical practitioners. The company was founded in 1932 by Henry Schein and is headquartered in Melville, New York. Henry Schein operates through two business segments: healthcare distribution and technology and value-added services. The company distributes a wide range of products and services, including medical, dental, and veterinary supplies, equipment, and technology solutions.

In the 1950s, Henry Schein began to expand its product offerings to include medical and dental supplies. In the 1970s, the company began to focus on providing value-added services to its customers, such as consulting and training. In the 1980s, Henry Schein began to expand its international presence, opening offices in Europe, Asia, and Latin America. Today, Henry Schein is a global leader in the health care industry, with operations in more than 30 countries.
