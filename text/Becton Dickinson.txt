===Introduction to Becton Dickinson===
Becton Dickinson (BD) is a global medical technology company that manufactures and sells medical supplies, devices, laboratory equipment, and diagnostic products. Founded in 1897, BD is headquartered in Franklin Lakes, New Jersey and has operations in more than 50 countries. The company is a leader in the medical technology industry, with a portfolio of products and services that span the entire healthcare continuum. BD is committed to improving healthcare outcomes and providing innovative solutions to help healthcare professionals and patients around the world.
===History of Becton Dickinson===
BD was founded in 1897 by Maxwell Becton and Fairleigh Dickinson. The company began as a manufacturer of medical syringes and needles, and has since grown to become a leader in the medical technology industry. Over the years, BD has acquired numerous companies and expanded its product portfolio to include a wide range of medical supplies, devices, laboratory equipment, and diagnostic products. BD has also developed a strong presence in the healthcare IT market, with products and services that help healthcare providers manage patient data and improve patient care.
