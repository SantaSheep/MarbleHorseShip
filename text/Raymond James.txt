===Introduction to Raymond James===
Raymond James is a financial services firm that provides a wide range of services to individuals, corporations, and institutions. Founded in 1962, Raymond James is one of the largest independent financial services firms in the United States. The company is headquartered in St. Petersburg, Florida and has over 8,000 financial advisors and over 2,000 locations throughout the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom. Raymond James provides a variety of services, including [[Investment Banking|investment banking]], [[Asset Management|asset management]], private client services, and [[Capital Markets|capital markets]].
===History of Raymond James===
Raymond James was founded in 1962 by Robert A. James and his son, Tom James. The company was initially a regional brokerage firm, but quickly grew to become one of the largest independent financial services firms in the United States. In the early years, Raymond James focused on providing services to individuals and small businesses. Over the years, the company has expanded its services to include investment banking, asset management, private client services, and capital markets.
