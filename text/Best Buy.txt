===Introduction to Best Buy===

Best Buy is a multinational consumer electronics retailer that operates in the United States, Canada, Mexico, China, and Turkey. Founded in 1966, the company has grown to become one of the largest electronics retailers in the world, with over 1,000 stores in the United States alone. Best Buy is known for its wide selection of electronics, including computers, televisions, gaming consoles, and home appliances. The company also offers a variety of services, such as installation, repair, and delivery. Best Buy is a publicly traded company, listed on the New York Stock Exchange under the ticker symbol BBY.
===History of Best Buy===

Best Buy was founded in 1966 by Richard Schulze and Gary Smoliak in St. Paul, Minnesota. The company began as an audio specialty store, selling stereo equipment and other electronics. In 1983, the company changed its name to Best Buy and began to expand its product offerings to include televisions, computers, and other consumer electronics. In the 1990s, Best Buy began to expand its store locations, opening stores in Canada, Mexico, and China. In the 2000s, the company began to focus on its online presence, launching its website in 2000 and expanding its online offerings.
