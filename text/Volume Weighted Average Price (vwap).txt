===Introduction to Volume Weighted Average Price (VWAP) === 
Volume Weighted Average Price (VWAP) is a trading indicator used to measure the average price of a security over a specific period of time. It is calculated by taking the total dollar value of all trades in a given period and dividing it by the total number of shares traded. VWAP is a popular tool among traders and investors as it provides a reliable measure of the average price of a security.
