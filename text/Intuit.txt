===Introduction to Intuit===
Intuit Inc. is a financial software company based in Mountain View, California. Founded in 1983, Intuit has become one of the most successful and influential companies in the financial technology industry. Intuit’s products and services are used by millions of individuals, small businesses, and large corporations around the world. Intuit’s mission is to “power prosperity around the world” by providing innovative financial solutions that help people and businesses succeed.
===History of Intuit===
Intuit was founded in 1983 by Scott Cook and Tom Proulx. The company was initially focused on developing software for [[Personal Finance|personal finance]] and accounting. Intuit’s first product, Quicken, was released in 1984 and quickly became a best-seller. In the 1990s, Intuit expanded its [[Product Line|product line]] to include business accounting software, payroll services, and tax preparation software.
===Intuit’s Financial Performance===
Intuit has been a consistently profitable company since its founding. In 2019, Intuit reported total revenue of $7.2 billion, up from $6.7 billion in 2018. Intuit’s [[Net Income|net income]] for 2019 was $1.7 billion, up from $1.5 billion in 2018. Intuit’s stock price has also been steadily increasing over the past five years, reaching an all-time high of $323.50 in April 2020.
===Intuit’s Products and Services===
Intuit offers a wide range of products and services for individuals, small businesses, and large corporations. Intuit’s most popular products include Quicken, QuickBooks, TurboTax, and Mint. Intuit also offers payroll services, tax preparation services, and financial planning services.
