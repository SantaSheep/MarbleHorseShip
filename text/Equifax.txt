===Introduction to Equifax===
Equifax is a global information solutions company that provides data, analytics, and technology to businesses and consumers. Founded in 1899, Equifax is one of the oldest and most respected credit reporting agencies in the world. Equifax is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia and has offices in more than 24 countries. The company provides a wide range of services, including credit reporting, identity theft protection, fraud prevention, and [[Risk Management|risk management]]. Equifax also provides data and analytics to help businesses make better decisions and manage risk.
===History of Equifax===
Equifax was founded in 1899 by Cator and Guy Woolford as the Retail Credit Company. The company was initially established to provide credit information to retailers, but it quickly expanded to provide credit information to banks, insurance companies, and other businesses. In the 1950s, the company began to offer consumer credit reports and began to expand its services to include identity theft protection and fraud prevention. In the 1970s, Equifax began to offer credit scoring services and began to expand its services to include risk management and analytics.
