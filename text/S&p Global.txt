===Introduction to S&P Global===

S&P Global is a leading provider of [[Financial Market|financial market]] intelligence, data, and analytics. S&P Global provides a wide range of services, including ratings, indices, research, and analytics. The company is a subsidiary of S&P Global Inc., a publicly traded company listed on the New York Stock Exchange.
===History of S&P Global===

S&P Global was founded in 1888 as Standard & Poor’s Corporation. The company was initially established to provide investors with reliable information about the performance of stocks and bonds. Over the years, the company has grown and expanded its services to include ratings, indices, research, and analytics. In 2016, S&P Global was spun off from its [[Parent Company|parent company]], McGraw Hill Financial, and became an independent publicly traded company.
