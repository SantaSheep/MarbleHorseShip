===Introduction to Tjx Companies===

TJX Companies, Inc. is an American multinational off-price apparel and home goods retailer based in Framingham, Massachusetts. It is the [[Parent Company|parent company]] of TJ Maxx, Marshalls, HomeGoods, Sierra Trading Post, and Homesense. TJX Companies operates in the United States, Canada, Europe, Australia, and New Zealand. The company is the largest off-price retailer of apparel and home fashions in the United States, with over 3,000 stores in the United States and Canada.
===History of Tjx Companies===

TJX Companies was founded in 1956 as Zayre Corporation, a discount department store chain. In 1988, the company changed its name to TJX Companies, Inc. and began to focus on off-price retailing. In 1989, the company acquired Marshalls, a chain of off-price department stores. In 1994, the company acquired HomeGoods, a chain of home furnishings stores. In 1996, the company acquired Sierra Trading Post, an online retailer of outdoor apparel and gear. In 2017, the company acquired Homesense, a chain of home furnishings stores.
