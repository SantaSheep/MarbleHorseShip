===History of Motorola Solutions===

Motorola Solutions was founded in 1928 as the Galvin Manufacturing Corporation. The company was founded by brothers Paul and Joseph Galvin, who developed the first commercial car radio. In 1947, the company changed its name to Motorola, Inc. and began to focus on the development of two-way radios. In 2011, Motorola, Inc. split into two companies: Motorola Mobility and Motorola Solutions. Motorola Solutions focuses on mission-critical communications and analytics solutions.
