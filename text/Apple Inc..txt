===Introduction to Apple Inc.===

Apple Inc. is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California. It designs, develops, and sells consumer electronics, computer software, and online services. Apple is one of the world’s most valuable companies and is the largest technology company in the world by revenue. Apple is well-known for its iconic products such as the iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Watch.
===History of Apple Inc.===

Apple was founded in 1976 by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne. The company was initially focused on developing and selling personal computers. In the 1980s, Apple introduced the Macintosh, the first personal computer to feature a graphical user interface. In the 1990s, Apple launched the iMac, the first all-in-one computer. In 2001, Apple released the iPod, which revolutionized the way people listen to music. In 2007, Apple released the iPhone, which revolutionized the way people use mobile phones. In 2010, Apple released the iPad, which revolutionized the way people use tablets.
===Apple Inc.’s Financial Performance===

Apple has been consistently profitable since its inception. In 2019, Apple reported a revenue of $260.2 billion and a [[Net Income|net income]] of $55.3 billion. Apple’s stock price has also been steadily increasing over the years, reaching an all-time high of $233.47 in August 2018. Apple is also one of the most valuable companies in the world, with a [[Market Capitalization|market capitalization]] of over $1 trillion.
===Apple Inc.’s Products and Services===

Apple’s products and services include the iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Watch, Apple TV, Apple Music, Apple Pay, iCloud, and the App Store. Apple also offers a range of services such as AppleCare, AppleCare+, Apple Music, Apple News+, Apple Arcade, and Apple TV+.
===Apple Inc.’s Impact on the Economy===

Apple has had a significant impact on the global economy. Apple’s products and services have revolutionized the way people use technology, and its success has created thousands of jobs around the world. Apple is also one of the largest taxpayers in the United States, paying over $14 billion in taxes in 2019.

Apple Inc. is one of the world’s most successful and influential companies. It has revolutionized the way people use technology and has had a significant impact on the global economy.
