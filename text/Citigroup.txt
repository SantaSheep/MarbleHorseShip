===Introduction to Citigroup===
Citigroup is a global financial services company that provides a wide range of products and services to its customers. Citigroup is a diversified financial services company, offering a variety of products and services, including consumer banking, corporate banking, [[Investment Banking|investment banking]], [[Asset Management|asset management]], and insurance. The company is headquartered in New York City and is a component of the S&P 500 index.
===History of Citigroup===
Citigroup was founded in 1812 as the City Bank of New York. It was the first bank to be established in the United States and was the first to offer a checking account. In 1865, the bank changed its name to the National City Bank of New York. In 1955, the bank merged with the First National Bank of New York and changed its name to Citibank. In 1998, Citibank merged with Travelers Group to form Citigroup.
