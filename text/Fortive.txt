Fortive is a diversified industrial technology company that designs, manufactures, and services professional and engineered products, software, and services for a wide range of industries. The company has a portfolio of well-known brands, including Fluke, Tektronix, Gilbarco, and Veeder-Root. Fortive is a publicly traded company on the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE: FTV).

Fortive was founded in 1999 by James  Lico and Steven R. Shaw. The company was initially a spin-off of the industrial conglomerate [[Danaher Corporation|Danaher Corporation]]. In 2016, Fortive acquired Veeder-Root, a leading provider of fuel management solutions.
