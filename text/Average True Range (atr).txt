===Introduction to Average True Range (ATR) === 
Average True Range (ATR) is a technical indicator used by traders to measure the volatility of a security. It is a measure of the average price range of a security over a given period of time. ATR is used to identify potential entry and exit points in the market, as well as to assess the risk associated with a particular trade. ATR can be used in conjunction with other technical indicators to help traders make more informed decisions.
===What is Average True Range (ATR)? ===
