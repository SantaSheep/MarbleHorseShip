===Introduction to ExxonMobil===
ExxonMobil is one of the world’s largest publicly traded international oil and gas companies. It is the largest of the six “supermajor” oil and gas companies in the world, and is the largest publicly traded company in the world by [[Market Capitalization|market capitalization]]. ExxonMobil is headquartered in Irving, Texas, and is a direct descendant of John  Rockefeller’s Standard Oil Company. The company operates in over 50 countries and has a presence in all of the world’s major oil and gas markets.
===History of ExxonMobil===
ExxonMobil was formed in 1999 through the merger of Exxon and Mobil, two of the world’s largest oil companies. Exxon was founded in 1870 by John  Rockefeller as the Standard Oil Company, and Mobil was founded in 1911 as the Socony-Vacuum Oil Company. The two companies merged in 1999 to form ExxonMobil, creating the world’s largest publicly traded oil and gas company.
