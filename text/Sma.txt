===Introduction to SMA===

Simple [[Moving Average|Moving Average]] (SMA) is a [[Technical Analysis|technical analysis]] tool used to identify trends in [[Financial Market|financial market]]s. It is a widely used indicator that is used to measure the average price of a security over a given period of time. The SMA is calculated by taking the average of the closing prices of a security over a certain number of periods. The SMA is a lagging indicator, meaning that it is based on past data and is not predictive of future price movements.
