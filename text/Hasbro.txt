===Introduction to Hasbro===
Hasbro, Inc. is an American multinational toy and board game company based in Pawtucket, Rhode Island. Founded in 1923, Hasbro is one of the largest toy companies in the world and is best known for its iconic brands such as Monopoly, G. Joe, and My Little Pony. Hasbro has a wide range of products and services, including toys, games, and entertainment. The company also produces television shows, movies, and digital content.
