===Introduction to Alternative Investments === 
Alternative investments are investments that are not traditional stocks, bonds, or cash. They are investments in assets such as real estate, commodities, private equity, hedge funds, and derivatives. Alternative investments are often used to diversify a portfolio and reduce risk. They can also provide higher returns than traditional investments.
