===Introduction to Additional Paid-In Capital === 

===Definition of Additional Paid-In Capital === 
How Additional Paid-In Capital is Recorded - Additional paid-in capital is recorded on the company's balance sheet as a separate line item. It is reported as a long-term asset and is not included in the calculation of the company's net income.
===How Additional Paid-In Capital is Recorded === 

===Examples of Additional Paid-In Capital === 
Disadvantages of Additional Paid-In Capital - The main disadvantage of additional paid-in capital is that it is not included in the calculation of the company's net income. This means that the company's profits are not affected by the amount of additional paid-in capital that it has. Additionally, additional paid-in capital is not tax deductible.
