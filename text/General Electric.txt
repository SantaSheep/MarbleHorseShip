===Introduction to General Electric===

Morgan, and has since grown to become a global leader in innovation and technology.
===History of General Electric===

GE was founded in 1892 by Thomas Edison and J.P. Morgan. Edison had previously founded the Edison Electric Light Company in 1878, and Morgan had been a major investor in the company. The two men merged their companies to form General Electric, which quickly became a leader in the electrical industry. In the early 1900s, GE began to diversify its business, expanding into the aviation, healthcare, and finance sectors. In the 1950s, GE was one of the first companies to develop nuclear power, and in the 1960s, it began to focus on developing new technologies such as computers and medical imaging. In the 1980s, GE began to expand its operations globally, and in the 1990s, it became a leader in the digital age.
