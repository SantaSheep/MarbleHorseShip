===Introduction to Lowes===

Lowe's Companies, Inc. is an American home improvement retailer that operates over 2,200 stores in the United States, Canada, and Mexico. Founded in 1946, Lowe's has grown to become one of the largest home improvement retailers in the world. Lowe's offers a wide variety of products and services, including home improvement supplies, appliances, tools, and services. Lowe's also offers a variety of services, such as installation, repair, and delivery. Lowe's is headquartered in Mooresville, North Carolina.
===History of Lowes===

Lowe's was founded in 1946 by Lucius Smith Lowe in North Wilkesboro, North Carolina. Lowe's began as a small hardware store, but quickly grew to become one of the largest home improvement retailers in the United States. In 1952, Lowe's opened its first store outside of North Carolina, in South Carolina. By the 1960s, Lowe's had expanded to over 100 stores in the Southeast. In the 1970s, Lowe's began to expand nationally, opening stores in the Midwest and West. In the 1980s, Lowe's began to expand internationally, opening stores in Canada and Mexico. Today, Lowe's operates over 2,200 stores in the United States, Canada, and Mexico.
