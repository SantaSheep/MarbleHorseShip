===Introduction to Phillips 66===

Phillips 66 is an American multinational energy company headquartered in Houston, Texas. It is a Fortune 500 company and is one of the largest refiners and marketers of petroleum products in the United States. Phillips 66 is a downstream energy company, meaning it is involved in the refining, marketing, and transportation of petroleum products. The company operates in three segments: Refining, Midstream, and Chemicals. Phillips 66 also has a retail segment, which includes its network of convenience stores and gas stations.
===History of Phillips 66===

Phillips 66 was founded in 1917 by Frank Phillips and E. Phillips as the Phillips Petroleum Company. The company was initially focused on oil exploration and production, but it soon expanded into refining and marketing. In 1929, the company merged with the Texas Company, forming the Phillips Petroleum and Texas Company. The company changed its name to Phillips 66 in 1973 and has since grown to become one of the largest refiners and marketers of petroleum products in the United States.
===Business Model of Phillips 66===
