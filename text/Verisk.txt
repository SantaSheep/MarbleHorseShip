===History of Verisk===

Verisk was founded in 1971 by Dr. Robert E. Hart, a former professor of economics at the University of Pennsylvania. Hart had a vision of creating a company that could provide data-driven insights and risk management solutions to the financial services industry. He began by creating a database of insurance claims data, which he used to develop predictive models for insurers. Over the years, Verisk has grown to become a global leader in the financial services industry, providing data-driven insights and risk management solutions to a wide range of customers.
