===Examples of Income Per Capita === 
Conclusion - Income per capita is a measure of the average income earned per person in a given area. It is calculated by dividing the total income of a geographic area by its population. This measure is used to compare the economic well-being of different areas and to measure the progress of an area over time. Understanding income per capita can help to identify areas of economic inequality and to target resources to those areas.
