===What is Cum Laude? === 
Cum Laude is a Latin phrase meaning “with honor” and is used to recognize academic excellence. It is typically awarded to students who have achieved a high level of academic success in their studies. Cum Laude is usually awarded to students who have achieved a grade point average (GPA) of 3.5 or higher. It is one of the highest honors a student can receive and is often seen as a mark of distinction.
