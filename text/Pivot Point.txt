===Calculating Pivot Points === 
Using Pivot Points in Trading - Pivot points can be used to identify potential support and resistance levels in the market. Traders can use these levels to determine entry and exit points for their trades. Additionally, pivot points can be used to identify potential trend reversals.

===Examples of Pivot Point Strategies === 
• Breakout Trading – This strategy involves trading when the price breaks out of the range of the pivot point.

===Conclusion === 
Conclusion - Pivot points are a useful technical analysis tool that can be used to identify potential support and resistance levels, potential trend reversals, and potential entry and exit points.
