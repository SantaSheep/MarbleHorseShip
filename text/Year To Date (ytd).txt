===Introduction to Year to Date (YTD) === 
Year to date (YTD) is a financial term used to describe the period of time from the beginning of the current calendar year up to the present day. It is used to measure the performance of investments, businesses, and other entities over a specific period of time. YTD is a useful tool for investors, business owners, and other financial professionals to track progress and make informed decisions.
===What is YTD? === 
YTD is a financial term used to measure the performance of investments, businesses, and other entities over a specific period of time. It is calculated by taking the total amount of money earned or spent during the current calendar year and subtracting the amount earned or spent in the previous year. This calculation gives investors and business owners an accurate picture of their performance over the course of the year.
