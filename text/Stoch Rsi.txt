===Introduction to Stochrsi===

StochRSI is a technical indicator used in the analysis of [[Financial Market|financial market]]s. It is a momentum oscillator that measures the level of the RSI (Relative Strength Index) relative to its historical range over a set period of time. The StochRSI was developed by Tushar Chande and Stanley Kroll in the early 1990s and is used to identify overbought and oversold conditions in the market.

===Calculating Stochrsi===

The StochRSI is calculated by taking the current RSI value and subtracting the lowest RSI value over the lookback period. This value is then divided by the difference between the highest and lowest RSI values over the lookback period. The result is then multiplied by 100 to give the StochRSI value.
