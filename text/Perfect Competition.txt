===Introduction to Perfect Competition === 
Perfect competition is a market structure in which there are many buyers and sellers, all of whom are price takers. This means that each individual buyer and seller has no influence over the price of the product or service being sold. Perfect competition is a theoretical market structure that is rarely seen in the real world, but it is an important concept to understand in economics.
