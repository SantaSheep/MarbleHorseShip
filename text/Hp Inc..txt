===Introduction to HP Inc.===

HP Inc. is a global technology company that provides products, technologies, software, solutions, and services to consumers, small and medium-sized businesses, and large enterprises. HP Inc. was formed in 2015 when Hewlett-Packard Company split into two separate entities, HP Inc. and [[Hewlett Packard Enterprise|Hewlett Packard Enterprise]]. HP Inc. is headquartered in Palo Alto, California and is a publicly traded company on the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE) under the ticker symbol HPQ.
===History of HP Inc.===

HP Inc. was founded in 1939 by Bill Hewlett and Dave Packard in a Palo Alto, California garage. The company was initially focused on developing and selling electronic test and measurement equipment. In the 1950s, HP began to expand its [[Product Line|product line]] to include computers and calculators. In the 1980s, HP began to focus on the development of personal computers and printers. In the 2000s, HP began to focus on the development of enterprise solutions and services. In 2015, HP split into two separate entities, HP Inc. and Hewlett Packard Enterprise.
