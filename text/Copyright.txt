===Introduction to Copyright===
Copyright is a form of intellectual property law that grants exclusive rights to the creator of an original work. It is a legal concept that provides creators with the right to control how their work is used, reproduced, and distributed. Copyright law is designed to protect the rights of creators and to encourage creativity and innovation. Copyright law is an important part of the legal framework that allows creators to benefit from their work and to ensure that their work is not misused or stolen.
