===Introduction to John Deere===

John Deere is an American corporation that manufactures and distributes agricultural, construction, and forestry machinery, diesel engines, drivetrains used in heavy equipment, and lawn care equipment. Founded in 1837, John Deere is one of the oldest and most successful companies in the United States. The company is headquartered in Moline, Illinois and has operations in more than 160 countries. John Deere is the world’s largest manufacturer of agricultural equipment and is a major player in the construction and forestry industries.
===History of John Deere===

John Deere was founded in 1837 by blacksmith John Deere. Deere had moved to Grand Detour, Illinois from Vermont in 1836 and opened a blacksmith shop. He soon realized that the soil in the area was too hard for traditional plows, so he designed a steel plow that could cut through the soil more easily. This plow was a success and Deere soon began manufacturing and selling plows and other agricultural equipment.

In the late 1800s, John Deere began to expand its [[Product Line|product line]] to include tractors, combines, and other agricultural equipment. In the early 1900s, the company began to expand into the construction and forestry industries. By the mid-1900s, John Deere had become a major player in the agricultural, construction, and forestry industries.
