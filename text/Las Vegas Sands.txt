===Introduction to Las Vegas Sands===
Las Vegas Sands Corporation (LVS) is a global gaming and hospitality company based in Las Vegas, Nevada. It is the world’s largest casino company, with operations in the United States, Macau, and Singapore. The company owns and operates The Venetian Resort Las Vegas, The Palazzo Resort Las Vegas, and the Sands Expo and Convention Center in Las Vegas, as well as the Marina Bay Sands in Singapore and the Venetian Macao Resort Hotel in Macau. LVS is a publicly traded company listed on the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE: LVS).
===History of Las Vegas Sands===
Las Vegas Sands was founded in 1988 by Sheldon Adelson, a [[Real Estate|real estate]] entrepreneur. Adelson purchased the Sands Hotel and Casino in Las Vegas, which was the first property in the company’s portfolio. The company then expanded to Macau in 2004, and Singapore in 2010. In 2020, Las Vegas Sands announced plans to expand into Japan, with the opening of a casino resort in Yokohama.
