===Introduction to CBRE Group===
CBRE Group, Inc. is a Fortune 500 and S&P 500 company, and is the world’s largest commercial [[Real Estate|real estate]] services and investment firm. CBRE Group provides a broad range of services to clients in the real estate industry, including property sales and leasing, [[Capital Markets|capital markets]], project management, property management, and valuation and advisory services. CBRE Group operates in more than 80 countries and has more than 90,000 employees worldwide.
===History of CBRE Group===
CBRE Group was founded in 1906 as Coldwell Banker & Company by brothers-in-law Colbert Coldwell and Benjamin Banker. The company was initially a real estate brokerage firm, and it quickly grew to become one of the largest real estate firms in the United States. In the 1970s, the company began to expand its services to include property management, [[Investment Banking|investment banking]], and other services. In the 1980s, the company began to expand internationally, and in 1995, the company changed its name to CB Richard Ellis Group, Inc. In 2018, the company changed its name to CBRE Group, Inc.
