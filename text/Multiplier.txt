===Introduction to the Multiplier Effect === 

===How the Multiplier Effect Works === 
Examples of the Multiplier Effect: One example of the multiplier effect is when the government invests in infrastructure projects. This investment creates jobs and increases the amount of money circulating in the economy. Another example is when a business invests in new technology, which increases productivity and creates more jobs.
