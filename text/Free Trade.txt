===Examples of Free Trade Agreements === 
Although free trade has many benefits, it also has some challenges. One of the main challenges is that it can lead to job losses in certain industries, as countries may be able to produce goods and services more cheaply than other countries. Additionally, free trade can lead to increased inequality, as some countries may be able to benefit more from free trade than others. Finally, free trade can lead to environmental degradation, as countries may be tempted to produce goods and services in ways that are not environmentally sustainable.
