===How to Calculate SMA === 
Simple Moving Average (SMA) is a technical analysis tool used to identify trends in financial markets. It is a type of moving average that is calculated by taking the average of a certain number of past prices. The SMA is a lagging indicator, meaning that it follows the trend of the market rather than predicting it. It is used to smooth out short-term fluctuations and identify longer-term trends.
