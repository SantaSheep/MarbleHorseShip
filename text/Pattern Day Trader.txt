===Introduction to Pattern Day Trader===
Pattern day trading is a type of trading strategy that involves making multiple trades within a single day. This type of trading is often used by experienced traders who are looking to capitalize on short-term price movements in the market. Pattern day traders typically use [[Technical Analysis|technical analysis]] to identify patterns in the market and then use those patterns to make trades. Pattern day trading can be a very profitable strategy, but it also carries a high degree of risk.
===Definition of Pattern Day Trader===
A pattern day trader is an individual who makes four or more day trades within a five-day period. A day trade is defined as any transaction that involves the purchase and sale of a security within the same day. Pattern day traders must meet certain requirements in order to be considered a pattern day trader.
