===Introduction to Marathon Oil===

Marathon Oil Corporation is an American oil and gas exploration and production company headquartered in Houston, Texas. It is one of the largest independent oil and gas exploration and production companies in the United States. Marathon Oil is engaged in the exploration, production, and marketing of crude oil, natural gas, and natural gas liquids. The company operates in the United States, Canada, Europe, Africa, and the Middle East.
===History of Marathon Oil===

Marathon Oil was founded in 1887 as The Ohio Oil Company. The company was initially engaged in the production and sale of petroleum products. In 1930, the company changed its name to Marathon Oil Company. In 1982, the company was acquired by USX Corporation, which later became Marathon Oil Corporation. In 2011, Marathon Oil spun off its downstream business, [[Marathon Petroleum|Marathon Petroleum]] Corporation, and became an independent exploration and production company.
===Business Model of Marathon Oil===

Marathon Oil’s business model is focused on the exploration and production of oil and gas. The company has a portfolio of assets located in the United States, Canada, Europe, Africa, and the Middle East.
