===Introduction to Microsoft===
Microsoft is one of the world’s largest technology companies, with a presence in nearly every sector of the technology industry. Founded in 1975 by Bill Gates and Paul Allen, Microsoft has grown to become a global leader in software, hardware, and services. Microsoft’s products and services are used by millions of people around the world, and its [[Financial Performance|financial performance]] has been consistently strong over the years. In this article, we will explore the history of Microsoft, its business model, its financial performance, and its impact on the technology industry.
===History of Microsoft===
Microsoft was founded in 1975 by Bill Gates and Paul Allen. The two had met in high school and shared a passion for computers. They began developing software for the Altair 8800, one of the first personal computers. In 1980, Microsoft released its first operating system, MS-DOS, which quickly became the industry standard. Microsoft then released Windows, its graphical user interface, in 1985. This was followed by the release of Office in 1989, which quickly became the most popular office suite in the world.
===Microsoft’s Business Model===
Microsoft’s business model is based on the sale of software, hardware, and services. Microsoft’s software products include Windows, Office, and the Xbox gaming console. Microsoft also sells hardware such as the Surface tablet and the Xbox console. Microsoft’s services include [[Cloud Computing|cloud computing]], artificial intelligence, and machine learning. Microsoft also offers subscription-based services such as Office 365 and Xbox Live.
===Microsoft’s Financial Performance===
Microsoft’s financial performance has been consistently strong over the years. In the [[[[Fiscal Year|Fiscal Year]] End|fiscal year end]]ing June 30, 2020, Microsoft reported revenue of $143.02 billion, up 14% from the previous year. Microsoft’s [[Net Income|net income]] was $44.28 billion, up 19% from the previous year. Microsoft’s stock price has also been steadily increasing over the past five years, reaching an all-time high of $220.50 in July 2020.
===Microsoft’s Impact on the Technology Industry===
Microsoft has had a profound impact on the technology industry. Microsoft’s products and services have revolutionized the way people use computers and the internet. Microsoft’s Windows operating system is the most widely used operating system in the world, and its Office suite is the most popular office suite. Microsoft’s cloud computing services have enabled businesses to move their operations to the cloud, and its artificial intelligence and machine learning services have enabled businesses to automate processes and gain insights from data.
