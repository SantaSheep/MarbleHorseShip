===Calculating Distribution Yield === 
Advantages of Distribution Yield - Distribution yield is a useful metric for investors to consider when evaluating the potential return of an investment. It provides a more accurate measure of the income generated by a security than other metrics, such as price-to-earnings ratio or dividend yield. Additionally, it can be used to compare the income generated by different securities, allowing investors to make more informed decisions.

===Disadvantages of Distribution Yield === 
Conclusion - Distribution yield is a useful metric for investors to consider when evaluating the potential return of an investment. It provides a more accurate measure of the income generated by a security than other metrics, such as price-to-earnings ratio or dividend yield. However, it does not take into account the potential capital gains of a security or the potential risks associated with an investment.
