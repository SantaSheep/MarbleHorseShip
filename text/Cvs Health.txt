===History of CVS Health===

CVS Health was founded in 1963 as Consumer Value Stores. The company was founded by brothers Stanley and Sidney Goldstein and Ralph Hoagland. The company began as a small chain of health and beauty stores in the Northeast. Over the years, CVS Health has grown to become one of the largest pharmacy health care providers in the United States. In 1996, CVS Health acquired the pharmacy benefit management company Caremark, which allowed the company to expand its services to include pharmacy benefit management. In 2007, CVS Health acquired the pharmacy chain Longs Drugs, which further expanded its retail presence.
