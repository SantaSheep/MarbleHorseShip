===Introduction to Capital Account===

The capital account is a financial term that refers to the net balance of a country’s international transactions. It is a component of a country’s balance of payments, which is a record of all economic transactions between a country and the rest of the world. The capital account is composed of two parts: the financial account and the capital account. The financial account records all transactions related to the purchase and sale of [[Financial Asset|financial asset]]s, while the capital account records all transactions related to the purchase and sale of physical assets. The capital account is an important indicator of a country’s economic health and can be used to assess the strength of its currency.
