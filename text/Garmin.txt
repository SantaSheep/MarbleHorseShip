===Introduction to Garmin===

Garmin is a multinational technology company that designs, manufactures, and markets a wide range of consumer, automotive, and aviation products and services. Founded in 1989, the company is headquartered in Schaffhausen, Switzerland and has offices in the United States, Europe, and Asia. Garmin is best known for its GPS navigation and communication products, including GPS receivers, GPS watches, and GPS-enabled smartphones. The company also produces a variety of other products, including fitness trackers, action cameras, and marine electronics.
===History of Garmin===

Garmin was founded in 1989 by Gary Burrell and Min Kao. The two had previously worked together at a company called King Radio, where they developed the first GPS receiver for the aviation industry. After leaving King Radio, Burrell and Kao founded Garmin and began developing GPS receivers for the automotive and marine markets.
