===Introduction to Quarter-to-Date (QTD) === 
Quarter-to-date (QTD) is a financial term used to describe the performance of a company or investment over a specific period of time. It is a useful tool for tracking the progress of a company or investment over a three-month period. QTD is often used to compare the performance of a company or investment to the same period in the previous year.
===What is QTD? === 
QTD is a financial term used to measure the performance of a company or investment over a three-month period. It is calculated by taking the total amount of income or expenses for the current quarter and subtracting the total amount of income or expenses for the same period in the previous year.
