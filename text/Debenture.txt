===Introduction to Debentures === 
Debentures are a type of debt instrument used by companies to raise capital. They are issued by the company and are backed by the company’s assets. Debentures are a form of loan that is secured by the company’s assets and are usually issued for a fixed period of time. Debentures are a popular way for companies to raise capital as they offer a fixed rate of return and are relatively low risk.
