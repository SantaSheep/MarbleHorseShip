Nike,  was founded in 1964 by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight as Blue Ribbon Sports. The company initially operated as a distributor for the Japanese shoe company Onitsuka Tiger (now ASICS). In 1971, the company changed its name to Nike,  and began to focus on the design and manufacture of its own products.
