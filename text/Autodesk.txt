Autodesk is a leading provider of 3D design, engineering, and entertainment software. ===History of Autodesk===

Autodesk was founded in 1982 by John Walker and 12 other co-founders. The company was initially focused on developing computer-aided design (CAD) software for the engineering and architecture industries. In the early 1990s, Autodesk began to expand its product offerings to include 3D animation and digital media tools. In the 2000s, Autodesk began to focus on developing software for the entertainment industry, including video games and movies. In recent years, Autodesk has continued to expand its product offerings to include cloud-based services and mobile applications.
