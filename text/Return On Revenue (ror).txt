===Introduction to Return on Revenue (ROR) === 
Return on Revenue (ROR) is a financial metric used to measure the profitability of a business. It is calculated by dividing the net income of a company by its total revenue. ROR is a useful tool for investors and business owners to assess the financial performance of a company and compare it to other companies in the same industry.
