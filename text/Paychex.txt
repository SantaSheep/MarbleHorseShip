===Introduction to Paychex===
Paychex is a leading provider of payroll, human resource, and benefits services for small and medium-sized businesses. Founded in 1971, Paychex has grown to become one of the largest payroll and HR services providers in the United States, serving more than 600,000 clients. Paychex provides a comprehensive suite of services, including payroll processing, tax filing, employee benefits, and human resource management. Paychex is headquartered in Rochester, New York, and has offices in more than 100 locations across the United States.
===History of Paychex===
Paychex was founded in 1971 by Tom Golisano and Robert  Cavanaugh. Golisano and Cavanaugh had previously worked together at a payroll processing company, and decided to start their own business. The company was initially called Payroll Services of Rochester, and was focused on providing payroll services to small businesses. In 1979, the company changed its name to Paychex, Inc. and began offering additional services, such as human resource management and employee benefits.
===Paychex Services===
Paychex offers a wide range of services to its clients. The company’s core services include payroll processing, tax filing, and employee benefits. Paychex also offers human resource management services, such as employee onboarding, [[Performance Management|performance management]], and compliance. In addition, Paychex provides a suite of online tools, such as time and attendance tracking, employee self-service, and reporting.
===Paychex Financials===
Paychex is a publicly traded company, listed on the NASDAQ stock exchange under the ticker symbol PAYX. The company reported total revenue of $3.3 billion in [[Fiscal Year|fiscal year]] 2020, up from $3.1 billion in fiscal year 2019. Paychex reported [[Net Income|net income]] of $717 million in fiscal year 2020, up from $664 million in fiscal year 2019.
===Paychex Leadership===
Paychex is led by President and CEO Martin Mucci. Mucci has been with the company since 2004 and has served as President and CEO since 2011. The company’s [[Board Of Directors|board of directors]] is chaired by Gail  Fosler, who has been a director since 2004.
