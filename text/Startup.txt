===Benefits of Starting a Business === 
Startups are businesses that are in the early stages of development. They are typically characterized by a lack of resources, a small team, and a limited budget. Startups are often founded by entrepreneurs who have identified a need in the market and are looking to capitalize on it. Startups are typically funded by venture capitalists, angel investors, or other sources of capital.
