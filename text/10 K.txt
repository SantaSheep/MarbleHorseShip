===Introduction to 10 K===

The 10 K is a financial document that is required to be filed annually by publicly traded companies with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC). It is a comprehensive report that provides a detailed overview of a company’s [[Financial Performance|financial performance]] and position. The 10 K is an important document for investors, as it provides them with a comprehensive view of a company’s financial health.
