===Introduction to Yearly Renewable Term Plan of Reinsurance===

Yearly Renewable Term Plan of Reinsurance (YRTPR) is a type of reinsurance contract that provides coverage for a specific period of time, usually one year. YRTPR is a type of reinsurance that is renewable each year, meaning that the insurer can renew the contract for another year if they choose to do so. This type of reinsurance is beneficial for both the insurer and the reinsurer, as it allows the insurer to spread out their risk over a longer period of time and the reinsurer to collect premiums for a longer period of time.
