===Calculating Yearly Probability of Dying === 
There are several factors that can affect the yearly probability of dying. These include age, gender, lifestyle, and health status. Age is a major factor, as the probability of dying increases with age. Gender is also a factor, as men typically have a higher probability of dying than women. Lifestyle factors such as smoking, drinking, and diet can also affect the probability of dying. Finally, health status is a major factor, as those with chronic illnesses or other health conditions have a higher probability of dying.
