===Introduction to Return on Average Capital Employed (ROACE) === 

===Calculating ROACE === 
Benefits of ROACE: ROACE is a useful metric for investors and analysts to assess the profitability of a company. It is a good indicator of how efficiently a company is using its capital to generate profits. It also helps to compare the performance of different companies in the same industry.

===Limitations of ROACE === 
Conclusion: ROACE is a useful financial metric for investors and analysts to assess the profitability of a company. It is a good indicator of how efficiently a company is using its capital to generate profits. However, it does not take into account the risk associated with the capital employed or the cost of capital, which can have a significant impact on the profitability of a company.
