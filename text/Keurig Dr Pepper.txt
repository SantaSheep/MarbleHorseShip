===Introduction to Keurig Dr Pepper===

Keurig Dr Pepper (KDP) is a leading beverage company in North America. It is the result of a merger between Keurig Green Mountain and Dr Pepper Snapple Group in 2018. KDP has a portfolio of more than 125 brands, including iconic names such as Dr Pepper, 7UP, Snapple, A&W, and Sunkist. KDP is headquartered in Burlington, Massachusetts and has operations in more than 30 countries.
