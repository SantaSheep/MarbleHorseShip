===Introduction to Service Charges === 
Service charges are fees that are charged for services rendered. They are typically associated with banking, credit card, and other financial services. Service charges are typically assessed on a monthly or annual basis and are used to cover the cost of providing the service. Service charges can also be used to cover the cost of processing transactions, maintaining accounts, and providing customer service.
