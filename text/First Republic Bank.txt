===Introduction to First Republic Bank===

First Republic Bank is a leading [[Financial Institution|financial institution]] that provides a wide range of banking and [[Wealth Management|wealth management]] services to individuals, families, businesses, and non-profit organizations. First Republic Bank is a member of the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC) and is a publicly traded company on the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE: FRC).
===History of First Republic Bank===

First Republic Bank was founded in 1985 by a group of entrepreneurs led by James Herbert, who had previously founded the [[Investment Banking|investment banking]] firm Herbert & Company. The bank was initially established as a private bank, providing banking services to high-net-worth individuals and families. In the early 1990s, the bank began to expand its services to include [[Commercial Bank|commercial bank]]ing, wealth management, and trust services. In the late 1990s, First Republic Bank began to expand its presence in the United States, opening branches in California, New York, and other states.
