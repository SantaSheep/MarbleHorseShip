===Introduction to Cognizant===

Cognizant is a leading global professional services company that provides consulting, technology, and outsourcing services. Founded in 1994, Cognizant has grown to become one of the world’s largest IT services companies, with over 200,000 employees in more than 50 countries. Cognizant’s services span across industries, including banking and financial services, healthcare, manufacturing, retail, and media and entertainment. The company’s mission is to help clients “transform their businesses and create value through the use of technology.”
===History of Cognizant===

Cognizant was founded in 1994 by Lakshmi Narayanan and Francisco ’Souza. The company was initially called “Cognizant Technology Solutions” and was focused on providing IT services to the banking and financial services industry. In 1997, the company changed its name to “Cognizant Corporation” and began to expand its services to other industries. In 2000, Cognizant went public and began to expand its operations globally. Today, Cognizant is one of the world’s largest IT services companies, with over 200,000 employees in more than 50 countries.
===Business Model of Cognizant===

Cognizant’s business model is based on providing consulting, technology, and outsourcing services to its clients. The company’s services span across industries, including banking and financial services, healthcare, manufacturing, retail, and media and entertainment. Cognizant’s services are divided into four main categories: Consulting, Technology, Outsourcing, and Business Process Services. Consulting services include strategy and operations consulting, digital transformation, and customer experience consulting. Technology services include application development and maintenance, [[Cloud Computing|cloud computing]], and data and analytics. Outsourcing services include infrastructure management, application management, and business process services. Business process services include finance and accounting, [[Human Resources|human resources]], and [[Customer Service|customer service]].
