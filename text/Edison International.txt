===Introduction===

Edison International is a publicly traded energy [[Holding Company|holding company]] based in Rosemead, California. The company is committed to providing reliable, affordable, and clean energy to its customers.
===History of Edison International===

Edison International was founded in 1984 as a holding company for Southern California Edison. Since then, Edison International has grown to become one of the largest electric utilities in the United States.
