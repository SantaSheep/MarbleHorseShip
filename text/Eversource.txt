Eversource is an energy company that provides electricity, natural gas, and water services to customers in Connecticut, Massachusetts, and New Hampshire. ===History of Eversource===

Eversource was founded in 1996 as Northeast Utilities, a merger of Connecticut Light and Power, Yankee Gas, and Public Service Company of New Hampshire. The company was renamed Eversource in 2015 to better reflect its commitment to providing reliable energy services to its customers. Since its founding, Eversource has grown to become the largest energy provider in New England, serving more than 3.6 million electric and natural gas customers in Connecticut, Massachusetts, and New Hampshire.
