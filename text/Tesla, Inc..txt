Tesla,  is an American electric vehicle and clean energy company based in Palo Alto, California. Founded in 2003 by engineers Martin Eberhard and Marc Tarpenning, Tesla is now one of the world’s leading producers of electric vehicles and clean energy products. Tesla designs, manufactures, and sells electric cars, solar panels, and energy storage solutions. The company also provides energy services, including charging infrastructure, energy storage, and energy management. Tesla is committed to accelerating the world’s transition to sustainable energy and has become a leader in the electric vehicle industry.

History of Tesla, 

Tesla was founded in 2003 by engineers Martin Eberhard and Marc Tarpenning. The company was initially funded by a group of investors, including Elon Musk, who later became the company’s CEO. Tesla’s first product was the Tesla Roadster, an electric sports car that was released in 2008. The Roadster was the first production electric vehicle to use lithium-ion battery cells.

In 2010, Tesla released the Model S, its first mass-market electric vehicle. The Model S was the first electric vehicle to have a range of over 200 miles on a single charge. In 2012, Tesla released the Model , an all-electric SUV. In 2015, Tesla released the Model 3, its first mass-market electric sedan.
