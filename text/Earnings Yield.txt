===Calculating Earnings Yield === 
Benefits of Earnings Yield: The earnings yield is a useful tool for investors to compare the profitability of different companies. It can also be used to compare the profitability of a company to its peers in the same industry. The higher the earnings yield, the more profitable the company is relative to its peers.

===Risks of Earnings Yield === 
Conclusion: Earnings yield is a useful tool for investors to compare the profitability of different companies. It is important to remember that it is only one measure of a company's profitability and does not take into account other factors such as debt, cash flow, and other financial metrics.
