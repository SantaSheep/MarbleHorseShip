===Introduction to Residual Value === 

===Calculating Residual Value === 
Examples of Residual Value - Examples of assets with residual value include cars, trucks, boats, and real estate. The residual value of a car is the estimated value of the car at the end of its useful life. The residual value of a truck is the estimated value of the truck at the end of its useful life. The residual value of a boat is the estimated value of the boat at the end of its useful life. The residual value of real estate is the estimated value of the property at the end of its useful life.
