===Introduction to Applied Materials===

Applied Materials, Inc. is a global leader in the design, manufacture, and supply of advanced materials and equipment used in the semiconductor, display, and solar industries. Founded in 1967, the company has grown to become one of the largest suppliers of semiconductor equipment and materials in the world. Applied Materials has a presence in more than 30 countries and employs over 20,000 people. The company is headquartered in Santa Clara, California.
===History of Applied Materials===

Applied Materials was founded in 1967 by Michael A. McNeilly and James  Morgan. The company was initially focused on providing materials and equipment for the semiconductor industry. Over the years, the company has expanded its product offerings to include display and solar equipment and materials. In the early 2000s, Applied Materials acquired several companies, including Varian Semiconductor Equipment Associates, Inc., and Novellus Systems, Inc. These acquisitions helped the company to become a leader in the semiconductor industry.
===Business Model of Applied Materials===

Applied Materials operates a diversified business model that focuses on providing advanced materials and equipment for the semiconductor, display, and solar industries. The company’s products are used in the manufacture of semiconductor chips, displays, and solar cells. Applied Materials also provides services such as installation, maintenance, and repair of its products. The company’s customers include semiconductor manufacturers, display manufacturers, and solar cell manufacturers.
