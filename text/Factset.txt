===Introduction to Factset===

FactSet is a financial data and software company that provides financial information and analytics to the global financial services industry. Founded in 1978, FactSet has grown to become one of the world’s leading providers of financial data and analytics. The company’s products and services are used by investment banks, asset managers, [[Hedge Fund|hedge fund]]s, [[Private Equity|private equity]] firms, and other [[Financial Institution|financial institution]]s to make informed decisions. FactSet’s products and services include data, analytics, and software solutions that enable clients to access, analyze, and visualize financial data.
===History of Factset===

FactSet was founded in 1978 by Philip A. Hadley and Robert E. Goldsmith. The company was initially focused on providing financial data to the [[Investment Banking|investment banking]] industry. Over the years, FactSet has grown to become a leading provider of financial data and analytics. The company has expanded its product and service offerings to include data, analytics, and software solutions that enable clients to access, analyze, and visualize financial data. FactSet has also grown its customer base to include asset managers, hedge funds, private equity firms, and other financial institutions.
