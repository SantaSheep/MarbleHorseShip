===History of Marsh Mclennan===

Marsh Mclennan was founded in 1905 by Henry Marsh and Arthur Mclennan. The company began as a small insurance brokerage firm in New York City. Over the years, the company has grown to become one of the largest professional services firms in the world. In the 1950s, the company expanded its services to include consulting and investment management. In the 1980s, the company acquired several subsidiaries, including Mercer and Oliver Wyman.
