===Introduction to Ceridian===
Ceridian is a global [[Human Capital|human capital]] management (HCM) technology company that provides a range of services and solutions to help organizations manage their workforce. Founded in 1976, Ceridian has grown to become a leader in the HCM space, offering a comprehensive suite of products and services to help organizations manage their workforce. Ceridian’s products and services include payroll, benefits, time and attendance, workforce management, and more. Ceridian also provides consulting services to help organizations optimize their workforce and maximize their [[Return On Investment|return on investment]].
