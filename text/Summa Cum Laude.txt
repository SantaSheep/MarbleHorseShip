===Introduction to Summa Cum Laude===

Summa [[Cum Laude|Cum Laude]] is a Latin phrase meaning “with highest honors” and is used to recognize academic excellence. It is typically awarded to students who have achieved the highest level of academic excellence in their field of study. Summa Cum Laude is one of the highest honors a student can receive and is usually reserved for those who have achieved the highest level of academic excellence.
