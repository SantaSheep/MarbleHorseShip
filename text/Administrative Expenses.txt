===Introduction to Administrative Expenses === 
Administrative expenses are the costs associated with running a business or government organization. These expenses can include salaries, office supplies, rent, utilities, and other overhead costs. Administrative expenses are necessary for the day-to-day operations of any organization, and they can have a significant impact on the bottom line. Understanding how to manage these expenses is essential for any organization looking to maximize its profits.

There are several factors that can affect the amount of administrative expenses an organization incurs. ===Strategies for Managing Administrative Expenses ===
