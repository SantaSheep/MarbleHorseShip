===Introduction to Pretax Profit Margin === 
Pretax profit margin is a measure of a company’s profitability before taxes are taken into account. It is calculated by dividing the company’s pretax income by its total revenue. This metric is used to assess a company’s ability to generate profits from its operations and is an important indicator of a company’s financial health.
