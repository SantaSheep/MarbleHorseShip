===Introduction to Royal Caribbean Group===

Royal Caribbean Group is a global cruise company that operates a portfolio of cruise brands, including Royal Caribbean International, Celebrity Cruises, Azamara, and Silversea Cruises. The company is headquartered in Miami, Florida and is the world’s second-largest cruise operator, with a fleet of over 50 ships and a presence in over 20 countries. Royal Caribbean Group is a publicly traded company listed on the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE: RCL).
===History of Royal Caribbean Group===

Royal Caribbean Group was founded in 1968 by three Norwegian shipping companies, Anders Wilhelmsen & Company,  Skaugen & Company, and Gotaas Larsen. The company was initially known as Royal Caribbean Cruise Line and operated a single ship, the Song of Norway. Over the years, the company has grown to become one of the world’s largest cruise operators, with a fleet of over 50 ships and a presence in over 20 countries.
