===Introduction to Northern Trust===
Northern Trust is a global financial services firm that provides [[Asset Management|asset management]], [[Wealth Management|wealth management]], and banking services to individuals, families, and institutions. Founded in 1889, Northern Trust has grown to become one of the world’s leading financial services companies, with offices in 19 countries and more than $1 trillion in assets under management. Northern Trust is committed to providing clients with the highest level of service and expertise, and its focus on innovation and technology has enabled it to remain at the forefront of the industry.
===History of Northern Trust===
Northern Trust was founded in 1889 by Byron  Smith, a Chicago banker who wanted to provide a safe and secure place for his clients to store their money. Smith’s vision was to create a [[Financial Institution|financial institution]] that would provide a safe haven for its clients’ assets and provide them with the highest level of service. Over the years, Northern Trust has grown to become one of the world’s leading financial services companies, with offices in 19 countries and more than $1 trillion in assets under management.
