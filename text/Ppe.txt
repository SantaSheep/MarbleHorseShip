===Definition of PPE===

PPE includes land, buildings, machinery, equipment, furniture, fixtures, vehicles, and other physical assets that have a [[Useful Life|useful life]] of more than one year. PPE is considered to be a long-term asset because it is expected to be used for more than one year.
