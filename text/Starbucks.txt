===Introduction to Starbucks===

Starbucks Corporation is an American multinational coffee company and coffeehouse chain. Founded in 1971 in Seattle, Washington, Starbucks is now the largest coffeehouse company in the world, with over 30,000 stores in 78 countries. Starbucks is known for its high-quality coffee, espresso-based drinks, and pastries. The company also offers a variety of other products, including teas, juices, and snacks. Starbucks is a publicly traded company, listed on the Nasdaq stock exchange.
===History of Starbucks===

Starbucks was founded in 1971 by three partners: Jerry Baldwin, Zev Siegl, and Gordon Bowker. The company was originally named Starbucks Coffee, Tea, and Spice, and was located in Seattle’s Pike Place Market. The partners opened the first Starbucks store in Seattle in 1971, and the company quickly grew in popularity. By the 1980s, Starbucks had expanded to other cities in the United States, and by the 1990s, the company had opened stores in Europe, Asia, and the Middle East.

Today, Starbucks is the largest coffeehouse company in the world, with over 30,000 stores in 78 countries. The company has become a global brand, and is known for its high-quality coffee, espresso-based drinks, and pastries.
