===Introduction to Trimble Inc.===

Trimble Inc. is a global technology company that provides innovative solutions for a variety of industries. Founded in 1978, Trimble has grown to become a leader in the fields of positioning, navigation, surveying, construction, agriculture, and transportation. Trimble’s products and services are used in over 140 countries around the world. Trimble’s mission is to “transform the way the world works” by providing innovative solutions that enable customers to increase productivity, reduce costs, and improve safety.
===History of Trimble Inc.===

Trimble was founded in 1978 by Charles Trimble, a former engineer at Hewlett-Packard. Trimble initially focused on providing surveying and mapping solutions for the construction industry. In the 1980s, Trimble began to expand its product offerings to include GPS technology. In the 1990s, Trimble acquired a number of companies, including Spectra Precision, which enabled Trimble to expand its product offerings to include construction, agriculture, and transportation solutions. In the 2000s, Trimble acquired a number of companies, including Tekla, which enabled Trimble to expand its product offerings to include 3D modeling and visualization solutions.
