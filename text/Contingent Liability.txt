===Types of Contingent Liability === 
Examples of Contingent Liability - Examples of contingent liabilities include potential legal claims, guarantees, and other contractual obligations. Examples of legal contingent liabilities include potential lawsuits, product liability claims, and environmental claims. Examples of contractual contingent liabilities include guarantees, indemnifications, and other contractual obligations.

===Accounting for Contingent Liability === 
Conclusion - Contingent liabilities are potential liabilities that may or may not be realized depending on the outcome of a future event. Companies should identify and assess potential liabilities, monitor and manage existing liabilities, and develop strategies to mitigate potential liabilities. Companies should also consider purchasing insurance to cover potential liabilities.
