===Introduction to Off-Balance Sheet (OBS) === 
Off-balance sheet (OBS) is a term used to describe financial activities that are not reported on a company’s balance sheet. OBS transactions are typically used to reduce a company’s reported liabilities and assets, and can be used to manage a company’s financial risk. OBS transactions are often used to finance large projects, such as the construction of a new factory or the purchase of a new business.
