===Inc.===

NVR,  is a publicly traded homebuilding and mortgage banking company based in Reston, Virginia. The company was founded in 1980 and is currently one of the largest homebuilders in the United States. NVR operates in two segments: homebuilding and mortgage banking. The homebuilding segment is responsible for the construction and sale of single-family homes, townhomes, and condominiums. The mortgage banking segment provides mortgage banking services, including origination and servicing of mortgage loans. NVR is listed on the New York Stock Exchange under the ticker symbol NVR.
