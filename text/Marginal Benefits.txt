===Calculating Marginal Benefits === 
An example of marginal benefits is when a consumer buys an additional unit of a good or service. The additional benefit that the consumer receives from the additional unit is the marginal benefit. Another example is when an investor buys an additional share of stock. The additional benefit that the investor receives from the additional share is the marginal benefit.
