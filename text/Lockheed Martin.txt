===Introduction to Lockheed Martin===

Lockheed Martin is a global aerospace, defense, security, and advanced technologies company. It is the world’s largest defense contractor and the largest provider of IT services to the U.S. government. The company is headquartered in Bethesda, Maryland and employs over 110,000 people worldwide. Lockheed Martin is a leader in the development of advanced technologies, including space exploration, missile defense, and cybersecurity. The company has a long history of innovation and has been involved in some of the most important technological advances of the past century.
===History of Lockheed Martin===

Lockheed Martin was founded in 1912 as the Lockheed Aircraft Company. The company was initially focused on the production of aircraft for the U.S. military. During World War , the company produced the P-38 Lightning fighter plane, which was used by the U.S. Army Air Corps. After the war, the company continued to produce aircraft for the military, as well as for commercial use. In the 1950s, the company began to diversify its business and began to produce missiles and satellites. In the 1960s, the company merged with Martin Marietta to form the Lockheed Martin Corporation.
