===Introduction to Command Economy === 

===Advantages of Command Economy === 
A command economy is an economic system in which the government makes all economic decisions, including what to produce, how much to produce, and how to distribute the goods and services. This type of economy is also known as a centrally planned economy. In a command economy, the government controls the production and distribution of goods and services. The government sets prices, wages, and production goals. It also determines who gets what goods and services.
