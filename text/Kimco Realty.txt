===Introduction to Kimco Realty===
Kimco Realty Corporation is a [[Real Estate|real estate]] investment trust (REIT) that specializes in the ownership, management, and development of open-air shopping centers. Founded in 1966, Kimco Realty is one of the largest publicly traded REITs in the United States, with a portfolio of over 500 properties in 44 states, Puerto Rico, Canada, and Mexico. Kimco Realty is headquartered in New Hyde Park, New York, and is listed on the New York Stock Exchange under the ticker symbol KIM.
===History of Kimco Realty===
Kimco Realty was founded in 1966 by Milton Cooper and Martin Kimmel. The company was initially focused on the acquisition and management of shopping centers in the Northeast United States. Over the years, Kimco Realty has grown to become one of the largest publicly traded REITs in the United States. In the early 2000s, Kimco Realty began to expand its portfolio to include properties in other parts of the United States, as well as in Canada and Mexico.
