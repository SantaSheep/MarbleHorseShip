===Introduction to Commerce - What is Commerce?=== 
Commerce is the exchange of goods and services between two or more parties. It is the process of buying, selling, and exchanging goods and services, and involves the transfer of money and goods from one party to another. Commerce is a fundamental part of the global economy and is essential for the growth and development of any society.
===Types of Commerce - B2B, B2C, C2C=== 
Business-to-Business (B2B) commerce is the exchange of goods and services between two or more businesses. This type of commerce is often used to facilitate the production of goods and services, as well as to provide a platform for businesses to collaborate and share resources.
