===Introduction to Keysight===
Keysight Technologies, Inc. is a leading provider of electronic test and measurement solutions. The company was founded in 1939 as Hewlett-Packard Company and is headquartered in Santa Rosa, California. Keysight is a global leader in the design, manufacture, and delivery of electronic test and measurement solutions for the communications, aerospace, defense, and semiconductor industries.
