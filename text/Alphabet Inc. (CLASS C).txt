===Introduction to Alphabet Inc. (CLASS )===

Alphabet Inc. (NASDAQ: GOOG) is a multinational conglomerate headquartered in Mountain View, California. It was created in 2015 as a [[Holding Company|holding company]] for Google and its subsidiaries. Alphabet Inc. is the [[Parent Company|parent company]] of Google, YouTube, Android, and other subsidiaries. Alphabet Inc. is the world’s fourth-largest technology company by revenue and the second-largest publicly traded company in the world. Alphabet Inc. is a publicly traded company listed on the NASDAQ stock exchange under the ticker symbol GOOG.
