===Introduction to Valero Energy===
Valero Energy Corporation is an American Fortune 500 company based in San Antonio, Texas. It is one of the largest independent petroleum refiners and marketers in the world, with operations in the United States, Canada, the Caribbean, the United Kingdom, and Ireland. Valero is the world’s largest independent petroleum refiner and marketer, and the largest ethanol producer in the United States. ===History of Valero Energy===
Valero Energy Corporation was founded in 1980 by William E. Greehey and Robert F. Grady. The company was initially a small independent refiner and marketer of petroleum products in the United States. In the early 1990s, Valero began to expand its operations, acquiring several refineries and retail outlets in the United States and Canada. In 1997, Valero acquired Ultramar Diamond Shamrock, a large integrated oil and gas company, and in 2000, Valero acquired Premcor, a large independent refiner and marketer of petroleum products.
