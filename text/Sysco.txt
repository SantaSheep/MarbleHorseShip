===Introduction to Sysco===

Sysco Corporation is a global leader in the foodservice industry, providing food and related products to restaurants, healthcare and educational facilities, lodging establishments, and other customers who prepare meals away from home. Founded in 1969, Sysco is headquartered in Houston, Texas and operates in more than 200 countries and territories around the world. The company is the largest foodservice distributor in North America, with more than $60 billion in annual sales. Sysco is a publicly traded company, listed on the New York Stock Exchange under the ticker symbol SYY.
===History of Sysco===

Sysco was founded in 1969 by John Baugh and John F. Baugh, Jr. as a small, family-owned business in Houston, Texas. The company began as a foodservice distributor, providing food and related products to restaurants, healthcare and educational facilities, lodging establishments, and other customers who prepare meals away from home. Over the years, Sysco has grown to become the largest foodservice distributor in North America, with more than $60 billion in annual sales.
