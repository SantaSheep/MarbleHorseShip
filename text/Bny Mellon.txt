===Introduction to Bny Mellon===

Bny Mellon is a global financial services company that provides investment management, asset and [[Wealth Management|wealth management]], and other financial services to individuals, corporations, and institutions. ===History of Bny Mellon===

Bny Mellon was formed in 2007 through the merger of The Bank of New York and Mellon Financial Corporation. The Bank of New York was founded in 1784 and was the oldest banking institution in the United States. Mellon Financial Corporation was founded in 1869 and was one of the largest [[Asset Management|asset management]] firms in the world. The merger of the two companies created a global financial services powerhouse.
