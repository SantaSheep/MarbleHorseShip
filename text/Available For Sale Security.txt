===Introduction to Available-for-Sale Security === 
Available-for-sale securities are financial instruments that are bought and held by investors for the purpose of generating income or capital gains. These securities are typically traded on the open market and can include stocks, bonds, mutual funds, and other investments. They are considered to be a liquid asset, meaning they can be quickly converted into cash. Available-for-sale securities are a popular investment option for investors looking to diversify their portfolios and generate income.
