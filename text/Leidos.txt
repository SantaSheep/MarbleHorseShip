===History of Leidos===

Leidos was founded in 1969 as a spin-off of the defense contractor, Science Applications International Corporation (SAIC). The company was initially focused on providing engineering and information technology services to the U.S. government. In the early 2000s, Leidos began to expand its services to include logistics and other services. In 2013, Leidos acquired the defense contractor, DynCorp International, which further expanded the company’s capabilities. In 2015, Leidos acquired [[Lockheed Martin|Lockheed Martin]]’s Information Systems & Global Solutions business, which further expanded the company’s capabilities.
