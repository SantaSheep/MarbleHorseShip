===Introduction to Roper Technologies===

Roper Technologies is a diversified technology company that designs and develops software, medical devices, and industrial products. ===History of Roper Technologies===

Roper Technologies was founded in 1981 by George Roper, a former engineer at IBM. The company initially focused on developing software for the medical industry, but soon expanded into other areas such as industrial products and consumer electronics. In the 1990s, Roper Technologies acquired several companies, including the medical device manufacturer, Varian Medical Systems, and the industrial products manufacturer, [[Dover Corporation|Dover Corporation]].

In the 2000s, Roper Technologies continued to expand its operations, acquiring several companies in the software, medical device, and industrial products industries.
