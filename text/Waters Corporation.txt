===Introduction to Waters Corporation===
Waters Corporation is a leading global provider of advanced analytical and material science technologies and services. Founded in 1958, Waters Corporation has grown to become a leader in the field of analytical and material science technologies, providing innovative solutions to customers in the pharmaceutical, biotechnology, food, environmental, and industrial markets. Waters Corporation is headquartered in Milford, Massachusetts, and has operations in over 35 countries around the world.
===History of Waters Corporation===
Waters Corporation was founded in 1958 by Dr. Arnold O. Beckman, a renowned scientist and inventor. Dr. Beckman was the inventor of the pH meter, which revolutionized the field of analytical chemistry. In the early years, Waters Corporation focused on the development and manufacture of analytical instruments and related products. Over the years, Waters Corporation has expanded its product portfolio to include a wide range of analytical and material science technologies, including chromatography, mass spectrometry, and thermal analysis.
===Products and Services Offered by Waters Corporation===
Waters Corporation offers a wide range of products and services to its customers. The company’s product portfolio includes chromatography systems, mass spectrometers, thermal analysis systems, and related consumables and accessories. Waters Corporation also provides a range of services, including instrument maintenance and repair, software and data management, and training and support.
