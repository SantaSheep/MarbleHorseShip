===Introduction to Capitalization Rate === 
Capitalization rate, also known as cap rate, is a metric used to evaluate the potential return on an investment. It is calculated by dividing the net operating income (NOI) of a property by its current market value. The cap rate is used to compare the relative value of different investments and to determine the potential return on an investment.
