===Introduction to LTM===
LTM stands for “Last Twelve Months” and is a financial term used to measure the performance of a company over a period of time. It is a useful tool for investors and analysts to assess the financial health of a company and to make informed decisions about investing in the company. LTM is a measure of the company’s performance over the last twelve months, and is calculated by taking the average of the company’s financial results over the last twelve months.
===Definition of LTM===
LTM is a financial term used to measure the performance of a company over a period of time. It is calculated by taking the average of the company’s financial results over the last twelve months. This average is then used to compare the company’s performance over the last twelve months to its performance in previous periods. This comparison can be used to assess the company’s financial health and to make informed decisions about investing in the company.
