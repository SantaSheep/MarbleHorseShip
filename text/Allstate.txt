===Introduction to Allstate===

Allstate is one of the largest insurance companies in the United States. Founded in 1931, Allstate has grown to become a [[Fortune 100|Fortune 100]] company and one of the largest providers of personal lines insurance in the country. Allstate offers a wide range of insurance products, including auto, home, life, and [[Business Insurance|business insurance]]. Allstate also provides financial services, such as investments, retirement planning, and banking. Allstate is headquartered in Northbrook, Illinois, and has more than 10 million customers in the United States.
===History of Allstate===

Allstate was founded in 1931 by Sears, Roebuck & Co. as the Allstate Insurance Company. The company was initially created to provide auto insurance to customers of Sears. Allstate quickly grew to become one of the largest auto insurers in the United States. In the 1950s, Allstate began to expand its product offerings to include home, life, and business insurance. In the 1970s, Allstate began to offer financial services, such as investments, retirement planning, and banking.
