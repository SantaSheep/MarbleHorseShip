===Introduction to Oneok===
Oneok is a publicly traded energy company based in Tulsa, Oklahoma. It is one of the largest natural gas distributors in the United States, serving customers in Oklahoma, Kansas, Texas, and New Mexico. The company is a leader in the midstream sector of the energy industry, providing natural gas gathering, processing, storage, and transportation services. Oneok also owns and operates natural gas liquids (NGL) pipelines and storage facilities. The company is a Fortune 500 company and is listed on the New York Stock Exchange under the ticker symbol OKE.
===History of Oneok===
Oneok was founded in 1906 as Oklahoma Natural Gas Company. The company was acquired by ONEOK, Inc. in 1980 and changed its name to ONEOK, Inc. in 1981. Since then, the company has grown to become one of the largest natural gas distributors in the United States. In 2005, the company acquired the natural gas gathering and processing business of [[Duke Energy|Duke Energy]] Field Services, which significantly expanded its operations. In 2008, the company acquired the natural gas liquids business of Duke Energy Field Services, further expanding its operations.
