===Introduction to Market Value Added (MVA) === 
Market Value Added (MVA) is a financial metric used to measure the performance of a company. It is a measure of the value created by a company for its shareholders. MVA is calculated by subtracting the company’s total market capitalization from the total amount of capital invested in the company. It is a useful tool for investors and analysts to assess the performance of a company and compare it to its peers.
