===History of FedEx===

FedEx was founded in 1971 by Frederick W. Smith, who had the vision of creating a global transportation and logistics company. Smith had the idea of creating an overnight delivery service that could deliver packages from one city to another in a single day. The company began operations in 1973, and quickly grew to become one of the largest and most successful companies in the world. Today, FedEx is a global leader in the transportation and logistics industry, and is one of the most recognizable brands in the world.
