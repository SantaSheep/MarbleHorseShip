===Introduction to Exelon===

Exelon is a [[Fortune 100|Fortune 100]] energy company headquartered in Chicago, Illinois. It is one of the largest electric utilities in the United States, serving approximately 10 million customers in Illinois, Pennsylvania, Maryland, Delaware, New Jersey, and Washington,  Exelon is a diversified energy company that operates in three main business segments: generation, transmission, and distribution. The company is a leader in the energy industry, providing reliable and affordable electricity and natural gas to its customers. Exelon is also a leader in the development of clean energy sources, such as nuclear, wind, and solar power.
===History of Exelon===

Exelon was founded in 1887 as the Commonwealth Edison Company. The company was created to provide electricity to the city of Chicago. In the early 1900s, the company expanded its operations to include the states of Illinois, Pennsylvania, Maryland, Delaware, New Jersey, and Washington,  In 2000, Exelon merged with PECO Energy Company, creating one of the largest energy companies in the United States. In 2012, Exelon acquired [[Constellation Energy|Constellation Energy]], further expanding its operations.
