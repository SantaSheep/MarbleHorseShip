===Introduction to Parent Company===
A parent company is a company that owns enough voting stock in another firm to control management and operations by influencing or electing its [[Board Of Directors|board of directors]]. The parent company is often referred to as a [[Holding Company|holding company]] or a conglomerate. The parent company may be a publicly traded company, a [[Private Company|private company]], or a subsidiary of another company. The parent company is responsible for the overall management and direction of the subsidiary companies.
