===History of Western Digital===
Western Digital was founded in 1970 by Alvin B. Phillips and Kenneth H. Olsen. The company initially focused on the development of memory chips for the burgeoning personal computer market. In the 1980s, Western Digital began to focus on the development of hard disk drives, which quickly became the company’s primary product. In the 1990s, Western Digital began to expand its [[Product Line|product line]] to include solid-state drives, flash memory, and other storage solutions.
