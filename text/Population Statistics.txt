===Introduction to Population Statistics === 

===Overview of Population Growth === 
Overview of Population Growth: Population growth is the rate at which a population increases or decreases over time. It is influenced by a variety of factors, including fertility, mortality, migration, and economic development. Population growth can be measured in terms of absolute numbers or as a percentage of the total population.

Impact of Population Statistics on Government Policy: Population statistics are used to inform government policy. Governments use population data to understand the needs of their citizens and to develop policies that will improve the quality of life for their population.
