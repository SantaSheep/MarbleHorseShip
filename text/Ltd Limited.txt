===Introduction to Ltd Limited===
Ltd Limited is a type of business structure that is used in many countries around the world. It is a popular choice for businesses that want to limit their liability and protect their assets. Ltd Limited is a legal entity that is separate from its owners and is responsible for its own debts and liabilities. It is also known as a limited liability company (LLC) or a private limited company (PLC).
===Definition of Ltd Limited===
Ltd Limited is a type of business structure that is used in many countries around the world.
